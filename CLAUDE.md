# KillaBears Book Series

This repository contains the manuscripts and materials for the KillaBears book series, set in the world of Ursus.

## Project Overview

A series of books exploring the rich lore of the KillaBears universe, including:
- The Primal Age and the creation of Ursus
- The Five Great Spirits (Ursoth, Arcturus, Grimheart, Voidclaw, Elderfrost)
- The Primal Elements (Fire, Ice, Lightning, Nature, Void)
- The various species and tribes of Ursus

## World Reference

The lore is established in the Primal Kards website. Key source materials:
- `/mnt/d/KillaBears/Websites/Primal Kards Site/primal-kards/src/app/lore/page.tsx` - Main lore content

## Writing Guidelines

- Maintain consistency with established KillaBears lore
- Dark fantasy tone with primal/mystical elements
- Each book should be standalone but part of a larger narrative

## Key Themes

- The balance between the Five Elements
- The legacy of the Great Spirits
- Survival and adaptation in a harsh, magical world
- The bonds between different species and tribes
