# Book 1: TIMEFALL

## Overview

The first book in the KillaBears series, following Murphy "Mops" Grimsley - an accidental hero janitor - as he discovers a conspiracy, joins a rebellion, and accidentally gets sent back in time to when legends still walked the earth.

**Target Audience:** Ages 8-12 (Middle Grade)
**Format:** Heavily illustrated chapter book
**Tone:** Funny, adventurous, with genuine stakes

---

## Main Characters

### Murphy "Mops" Grimsley
- **Role:** Protagonist, janitor at Bearington Tower
- **Personality:** Anxious, sarcastic, in way over his head
- **Arc:** From invisible nobody to accidental rebel hero
- **Companion:** Tachyon (sentient alarm clock AI)

### Tachyon
- **Role:** Mops' KillaBit companion
- **Personality:** Time-obsessed, corrective, secretly ancient
- **Secret:** Design preserved for millennia by Order of Correction

### Dr. Fomo
- **Role:** Believed dead, actually alive and imprisoned
- **Importance:** Key to stopping Bearington's tyranny

### Bearington
- **Role:** Antagonist, tyrant ruler
- **Weapon:** Doomsday device (tampered with by Marshall)

### Omni Bear
- **Role:** Superhero who tries to stop the weapon
- **Fate:** Attempts to destroy weapon, triggers the TimeFall instead

### Ursoth the Shadowmaw
- **Role:** Legendary Fire Spirit (appears at end)
- **Setting:** Mops meets him in the deep past

## Plot Summary

### ACT 1: FOMO LIVES

**Chapter 1: The Invisible Bear**
- Introduce Mops as Bearington Tower's janitor
- Nobody notices him - he's furniture
- Show the oppressive Bearington regime
- Tachyon introduced as his only friend
- [ILLUSTRATION: Mops mopping while important bears walk past ignoring him]

**Chapter 2: The Message in the Mop Closet**
- Mops finds something he shouldn't - evidence Fomo is ALIVE
- Internal panic - what does he do with this info?
- [ILLUSTRATION: Mops' horrified face illuminated by a secret document]

**Chapter 3: Fomo Lives**
- Mops can't keep the secret - starts spray-painting "FOMO LIVES"
- Thinks he's being sneaky, actually terrible at graffiti
- The phrase starts spreading across the city
- [ILLUSTRATION: Terrible graffiti spelling "FOMO LIVSE" with Mops running away]

**Chapter 4: They're Watching**
- Mops realizes he's being followed
- Paranoia intensifies
- Cliffhanger: Someone grabs him in an alley

### ACT 2: THE REBELLION

**Chapter 5: The Rebels**
- Mops meets the underground resistance
- They've been looking for whoever started "Fomo Lives"
- Mops: "That was... intentional. Definitely a plan I had."
- [ILLUSTRATION: Scrappy rebel hideout with Mops looking terrified]

**Chapter 6: The Plan**
- Rebels explain: Fomo is being held in [LOCATION]
- They need someone invisible, someone no one notices...
- Mops realizes with horror they mean HIM
- [ILLUSTRATION: Rebels pointing at Mops while he points at himself in disbelief]

**Chapter 7: Training Montage (Gone Wrong)**
- Rebels try to train Mops for the mission
- He is catastrophically bad at everything
- But Tachyon has hidden talents...
- [ILLUSTRATION: Mops failing at obstacle course while Tachyon watches]

**Chapter 8: The Rescue Mission**
- Breaking into the facility
- Everything that can go wrong does
- But Mops' janitor skills actually help (knows all the service corridors)
- [ILLUSTRATION: Mops in air ducts with mop]

**Chapter 9: Finding Fomo**
- They find Dr. Fomo - he's real, he's alive
- But alarms are blaring
- Fomo warns them: "Bearington's weapon... it's almost ready"
- Cliffhanger: They're surrounded

### ACT 3: TIMEFALL

**Chapter 10: Bearington's Wrath**
- Bearington deploys the doomsday weapon
- Mass panic across the city
- Rebels scrambling, Mops and Fomo on the run

**Chapter 11: Omni Bear's Last Stand**
- Omni Bear arrives - the city's only hope
- Epic confrontation with the weapon
- Omni Bear attempts to destroy it
- [ILLUSTRATION: Omni Bear vs. massive weapon]

**Chapter 12: The Rip**
- The weapon doesn't explode - it TEARS
- Time itself rips open
- Bears, buildings, everything being pulled through
- Marshall's sabotage changed what the weapon does
- [ILLUSTRATION: Time rips across the sky, bears falling through]

**Chapter 13: Falling Through Time**
- Mops and Tachyon separated from everyone else
- Falling, falling, falling through impossible colors
- Everyone else vanishes toward one destination
- But Mops and Tachyon keep falling... further back
- [ILLUSTRATION: Mops screaming, reaching for others who disappear]

**Chapter 14: When Legends Walked**
- Mops wakes up in a world he doesn't recognize
- Primal. Ancient. VERY dangerous.
- Tachyon is glitching, confused about "when" they are
- Then Mops sees HIM...
- [ILLUSTRATION: Silhouette of massive bear with flames]

**Chapter 15: Ursoth**
- Face to face with Ursoth the Shadowmaw
- Not a legend. Not a story. REAL.
- Ursoth: "You smell like the future."
- Mops: [internal screaming]
- CLIFFHANGER ENDING
