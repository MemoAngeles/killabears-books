## Main Characters

### Murphy "Mops" Grimsley
- Janitor at Bearington Tower, invisible to everyone
- Sarcastic, pessimistic, accidentally heroic
- **Roommate**: Lives with Tachyon (they share a tiny apartment)

### Tachyon
- Sentient alarm clock AI, Mops's roommate and only friend
- Sharp, time-obsessed, secretly ancient
- **SECRET (not revealed in Book 1)**: Working with Marshall, manipulated events to cause TimeFall

### Dr. Fomo (Mentioned, Not Rescued)
- Secretly imprisoned (publicly "dead")
- Mops discovers he's alive but never reaches him
- **Humanizing detail**: Had a cat named Mrs. Fuddlesworth (shows his geeky, lovable side)
- Rescue is the GOAL but doesn't happen in Book 1

### Bearington
- Tyrant ruler, springs trap on the rebels
- Deploys doomsday weapon during street ambush

### Omni Bear
- Superhero who tries to stop the weapon, causes the TimeFall

### Ursoth the Shadowmaw
- Ancient Fire Spirit, appears at the end when Mops lands in the deep past

---

## Key Plot Change: The Betrayal

**What happens**: The rebels plan to rescue Fomo. Someone tips off Bearington. They're ambushed on the streets BEFORE reaching the building.

**Who betrayed them**: Tachyon (working with Marshall, the Order of Correction). Tachyon manipulated events to ensure the TimeFall would happen.

**When is this revealed**: NOT in Book 1. This is a mystery for later books. Readers may suspect something is off with Tachyon, but no confirmation.

**Clues planted in Book 1**:
- Tachyon suggests the "left corridor" (leads Mops to discover Fomo)
- Tachyon knows things he shouldn't
- Tachyon seems oddly calm during the chaos
- After TimeFall: "Murphy... I think I know why we're here" (hints at deeper knowledge)

**Illustrations:**

### ACT 2: THE REBELLION (Chapters 4-6)

*"You smell like the future. Like broken time. Like... hope."*

*Tachyon's screen flickered. "Murphy... I think I know why we're here. I think I've ALWAYS known. I think—"*

*But Ursoth smiled. And when an ancient spirit of war smiles, explanations have to wait.*

*"Come, little future-bear," Ursoth said. "We have much to do. And very little time."*

*He laughed at his own joke. I didn't get it yet.*

*I would.*

## The Betrayal Mystery (Series Arc)

### What Readers Know (Book 1):
- The rebels were ambushed—someone knew their exact route
- Tachyon seems to know things he shouldn't
- Tachyon is "oddly calm" and "unsurprised" at key moments
- Tachyon says "I remember this" when seeing Primal Ursus

### What Readers Don't Know Yet:
- Tachyon and Marshall (Order of Correction) orchestrated events
- The TimeFall was INTENTIONAL—a "correction" in the timeline
- Tachyon's design was preserved for millennia for this purpose
- Mops was specifically chosen as an "anchor" because he's unpredictable
