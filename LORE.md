### Economy (Thread Token System)

**Three Tiers:**
1. **Local:** Barter, reputation credit, gift economy
2. **Trade Goods:** Salt (universal), amber (luxury), tribal exports
3. **Thread Tokens:** Shaman-crafted, elemental glow, impossible to counterfeit

**Denominations:** Strand (1 week labor) → Weave (10) → Skein (100) → Tapestry (1000)
