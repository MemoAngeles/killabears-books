## New Lore Created

### Regions
- **Miktah** — Subterranean cavern system beneath Calaverna, bordering Ossuary March. DOD fur patterns = cavern maps. Miktah Keepers guide the dead.
- **Tarlands** — Coastal tribe at the Cinder Gulf. Tar pits, trade with seabears, war with goblin pirates. Chief Crusher wears ice grills as a power flex in volcanic heat.

### Suzuika Lore
- **Frost Suzuika** — White with blue spots, arrest molecular movement → freezing. Inverse of Fire. Full section added to `aliens_suzuika.md`.
- **Unified Telekinesis Framework** — All Suzuika use the same power at different scales (micro/macro/dimensional/gravitational).
- **Baby Fire Suzuika** — First companion Suzuika (Burlap Jim). Bonds can start young.

### Fur Genetics
- **Grey fur eyebrow scar** — Ursoth's sword wounded a grey bear so profoundly that every grey bear since is born with the scar. Added to `fur_genetics_system.md`.

### Mechanics
- **HP Caps:** OG KillaBears & BearBots = 25, Cubs & Bits = 20
- **Cursed Weapons:** Extra damage but drain wielder's life force
- **Weave-Eyes:** Cybernetic eye enhancement — AR, night vision, thermal, Thread-flicker detection (spiritual particles)
- **FreddyBear:** In-universe horror movie franchise (hockey mask tradition)

### Factions
- **Ski Mask Gang** — Outcasts, black market, underground. Hate Bearington, always show up when Ursus needs them.

### Book Changes
- **Digi → Louis "Lewie"** — Renamed across chapters 4-7 (51 references)
- **Honey Bear** — Updated to braids (from pigtails), blue eyes confirmed
