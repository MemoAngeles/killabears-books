## MAJOR LORE UPDATE: Fire Suzuika / Golden Eye Connection

Memo provided key lore details:

### Fire Suzuikas (Golden-Eyed Suzuikas)
- Called "Golden-Eyed" because their single eye is GOLDEN
- **Micro-telekinesis**: They excite molecules to create heat/fire (not magic!)
- Expel flammable liquid from tentacles, then ignite with telekinesis
- Impervious to fire
- Blue Suzuikas = MACRO-telekinesis (move objects); Fire = MICRO (move molecules)

### Mielikki (1-of-1 KillaCub)
- **Appearance**: Black fur, white hair, dark wizard aesthetic
- **BOTH eyes** are black with golden pupils
- **Theory**: She sacrificed her original eyes and made pacts with TWO Fire Suzuikas
- Each golden pupil = one Fire Suzuika looking out through her empty socket
- This explains dual eyes (Bearington only has ONE = one deal/theft)

### Bearington's Mutations = DIMENSIONAL
- Not purely genetic — possibly dimensional
- His experiments may have opened a rift to the Suzuika's parallel weaving
- The golden eye might be a literal Fire Suzuika presence (stolen, not earned)
- This explains his power AND potential instability

### Implications
- Mielikki earned her power through PACTS (honorable deals)
- Bearington gained his through FUSION (consumed/absorbed creatures)
- This is a critical plot difference!

## MAJOR PLOT DEVELOPMENT: Pact vs Fusion

### Mielikki's Character
- **Astronomer + witch** — researched cosmic origins of power on Ursus
- Found the Suzuikas, became obsessed, eventually made contact and pacts
- Made deals with TWO Fire Suzuikas (one per eye)
- **Weakness**: Eyes occasionally move independently (two separate entities)

### The Story Beat
1. **KillaBears vs Mielikki** — they face her (reason TBD), notice her eyes move independently, exploit this to defeat her

### Why It Works
- Subverts expectations built from Mielikki fight
- Shows Bearington is MORE monstrous (predator vs deal-maker)
- Mielikki: has rules, honor, separate entities
- Bearington: no rules, consumed everything, no seams to exploit
