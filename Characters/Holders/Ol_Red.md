## Appearance

Ol' Red looks like he just stumbled out of the wrong classroom and into a warzone — which is basically what happened.

Dark brown fur topped with an unmistakable red mullet — business in the front, party in the back, chaos everywhere. His eyes are a dizzy yellow, permanently spinning like he's three drinks in or just got hit by a truck. Maybe both. The Kenjōkai Academy uniform hangs on him slightly askew, like it was meant for someone who has their life together.

The "Dizzy Specs" perched on his nose don't help — they might actually make the dizziness worse, but he swears they're prescription.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Perpetually disoriented but somehow effective
- Late to everything, clutch at the last second
- Underestimated by everyone, which is his secret weapon
- Heart of gold buried under layers of "huh?"

**The Exchange Student:**
Ol' Red was sent to Kenjōkai Academy as an exchange student. Whether he graduated is... unclear. What IS clear is that he absorbed just enough training to be dangerous, wrapped it in a mullet, and showed up to the fight fashionably (aggressively) late. Every single time.
