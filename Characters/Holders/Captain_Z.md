## Appearance

Captain Z commands from beyond the grave. Green zombie fur, a captain's hat worn with authority that death couldn't strip away. A crystal embedded in his forehead pulses with faint light — the source of whatever keeps him thinking. One arm is stitched back on, held together by thread and sheer willpower.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Commands with quiet, undead authority
- The crystal gives him clarity other zombies lack
- Stitched together but never broken
- A captain before death, a captain after

**The Commander:**
Most zombies shamble. Captain Z strategizes. The crystal on his forehead preserved what matters — tactical thinking, leadership instinct, the iron will to hold a line. He doesn't remember everything from before, but he remembers how to lead.
