## Appearance

Killa Bone is a walking nightmare. His skull-patterned face strips away any illusion of friendliness — this is a bear built for violence and nothing else. A bullet belt slung across his chest isn't decorative; every round has been counted, and most have been spent.

His posture is hunched, feral, more animal than civilized bear. When he moves, it's with the twitching, unpredictable energy of something barely restrained.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Feral and primal
- Consumed by the berserker curse
- Cannot distinguish friend from foe in battle rage
- Savage loyalty — when lucid

**The Cursed Berserker:**
Killa Bone carries the curse of Ceta Ko — the berserker's affliction. Once battle begins, rage swallows him whole. He doesn't fight with strategy or precision. He fights like a cornered animal, all teeth and fury and destruction.

The problem? He doesn't stop. He doesn't recognize allies. When the red mist descends, everyone is an enemy. His friends have learned to stay clear when Bone starts twitching.

---

## The Ceta Ko Curse

The berserker curse of Ceta Ko is ancient and merciless:
- Triggers involuntarily at the scent of blood or sounds of combat
- Grants enormous strength and pain resistance
- Strips away higher reasoning — pure feral instinct
- Cannot differentiate between friend and foe
- Ends only when the body collapses from exhaustion

Killa Bone didn't choose this. It was done to him. And every time he wakes up surrounded by destruction, he has to ask: *Did I hurt someone I care about?*

## Combat Style

| Approach | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Entry** | Unleashed like a weapon — pointed at the enemy and released |
| **Method** | Pure feral savagery, no technique, all instinct |
| **Strength** | Unstoppable in rage, ignores pain, terrifying |
| **Weakness** | Cannot be controlled; allies must avoid him in combat |

Killa Bone is a strategic liability and a tactical nuke. You deploy him when you need something destroyed and you don't care about collateral damage.

---

## Role in the Killaverse

He's the weapon nobody wants to use but everybody needs. The bear-shaped bomb. Teams keep their distance when Bone is deployed — he's a one-bear wrecking crew, but the safety radius is measured in city blocks.

**Potential Story Role:**
A tragic figure — powerful beyond measure but cursed to hurt the things he loves. The berserker who fights harder than anyone but can never be trusted in close quarters. Does he seek a cure? Does he embrace the monster? That's his story.
