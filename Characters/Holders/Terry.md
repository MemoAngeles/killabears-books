## Appearance

Terry is the bear equivalent of a college professor who accidentally wandered into a warzone. White fur, an absolutely magnificent orange mullet, and nerdy glasses that he's constantly adjusting. He looks out of place in every combat situation because he IS out of place. His paws are stained with alchemical reagents, not blood.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Intellectual, curious, always experimenting
- Non-fighter — prefers solutions over violence
- The mullet is intentional and he will defend it
- Professor/alchemist type who makes things go boom (accidentally)

**The Alchemist:**
Terry doesn't fight. Terry *invents*. Potions, compounds, explosives (oops), healing salves, smoke bombs — if it can be mixed, brewed, or distilled, Terry's already tried it. His lab coat has more burn marks than a dragon's dinner plate.
