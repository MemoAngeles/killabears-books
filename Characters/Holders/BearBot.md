## Appearance

BearBot is steel and purpose given bear-shaped form. Red eyes glow from a metal skull, casting crimson light across a reinforced jaw that can bite through cable. Dr. Fomo's helpers come in many configurations — this one runs hot, looks mean, and moves with mechanical precision that organic bears can't match.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Loyal to programming — or to something deeper?
- Efficient, direct, no wasted movement
- May or may not be fully conscious (the question haunts him)
- Follows orders... until he doesn't

**The Question:**
BearBots can be conscious or not. This one? He follows orders a little too well, hesitates a beat too long before compliance, and sometimes stares at sunsets for no operational reason. Draw your own conclusions.
