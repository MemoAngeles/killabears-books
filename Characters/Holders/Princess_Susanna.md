## Appearance

Princess Susanna is pure magic given form.

Her fur is a complete rainbow gradient—purple at the top, through blue, green, yellow, orange, to pink at the bottom. She wears a delicate gold crown with a red gem centerpiece and blue gems on the sides.

Her eyes are usually peacefully closed, a serene smile on her face. She looks like the most precious plush toy ever made—complete with a small X-stitch mark on her cheek and stitched paws.

## Background

### The Rainbow Princess

Where did Princess Susanna come from? Some say she's a manifestation of hope itself—born when the Killaverse needed her most. Others say she was an ordinary cub whose kindness was so pure that the Thread itself rewove her into something magical.

She doesn't explain. She doesn't need to. She simply IS.

### Her Purpose

In a world of violence and tyranny, Princess Susanna protects the one thing Bearington can never fully corrupt: dreams.

Every child in Ursamarch who sleeps peacefully does so under her protection. Every nightmare deflected, every fear soothed, every moment of wonder preserved—that's her work.

---

## Role in the Killaverse

Princess Susanna represents hope and innocence—the reason worth fighting for. Perfect for:
- Symbol of what the resistance protects
- Contrast to the darkness of Bearington's regime
- Dream sequences and magical interludes
- Reminder that gentleness is its own kind of power

**Thematic Importance:**
If Tachyon is the philosophical antagonist asking "Is the Thread flawed?", Princess Susanna is the answer: "Look what the Thread can create when it weaves with love."
