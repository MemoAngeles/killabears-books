## Appearance

Fomo Jr. has the soft peach fur of someone who wasn't built for the front lines. He looks young, uncertain, constantly second-guessing his posture. But his eyes — his eyes are his uncle's eyes. Sharp. Calculating. Seeing patterns where others see chaos. He just doesn't believe they're worth anything yet.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Insecure — lives in Dr. Fomo's enormous shadow
- Incredibly smart but doesn't trust his own intelligence
- Wants to prove himself but terrified of failure
- Heart of gold buried under anxiety

**The Nephew:**
Being Dr. Fogerty Fomo's nephew is a blessing and a curse. The blessing: genius runs in the family. The curse: everyone expects genius, and Junior's convinced he's the exception. He's wrong. He just needs someone to tell him that — and mean it.
