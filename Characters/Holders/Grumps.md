## Appearance

Grumps is beige fury wrapped in a blindfold. He can't see — or won't see — and the permanent scowl on his face suggests he prefers it that way. A purple scarf whips behind him. His body is tense, always coiled, always ready to explode. KillaBear #984, and every digit of that number is earned.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Perpetually angry — it's not a mood, it's a lifestyle
- Blindfolded but fights better than bears who can see
- The grumpiness is real, but so is the loyalty
- Don't touch the scarf

**The Berserker:**
Grumps fights blind and mad. The blindfold isn't a handicap — it removes distractions. All that's left is sound, smell, instinct, and rage. Lots of rage.
