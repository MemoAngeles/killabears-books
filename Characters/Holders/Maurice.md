## Appearance

Maurice looks like he just left a party. Or is heading to one. Or IS one.

Beige fur, tongue out (always), and draped in a glorious pink feather jacket that has absolutely no business being on a battlefield — and yet here he is. The jacket flows and bounces with every movement, making him look less like a fighter and more like a disco ball with claws.

He doesn't care. That's kind of the whole point.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Life of every party, even the ones that are actually fights
- Unpredictable — even his allies don't know what he'll do
- Tongue out, brain on — smarter than he looks
- Taunts enemies into making mistakes

**The Brawler:**
Maurice wins fights by making opponents too confused or too angry to think straight. His Wildcard nature means every round is a surprise. His Feather Flourish is both an attack and a performance. His Taunt? Devastatingly effective because it's genuinely annoying.
