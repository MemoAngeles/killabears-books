## Appearance

Honey Bear is sunshine in a warzone. Yellow fur, bright blue eyes behind aviator glasses, and black braids (braids, not pigtails — she's particular about this). She wears a uniform that's slightly too big, rolled at the sleeves, ready for action. She's a cub, but she carries herself like someone who's already survived more than most adults.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Brave beyond her years — canon rebel in Book 1
- The braids are braids. Not pigtails. She will correct you.
- Blue eyes see hope where others see ruin
- Uniform means business — she chose this fight

**The Rebel Cub:**
Honey Bear didn't stumble into the resistance. She marched in, aviator glasses on, braids swinging, and asked what needed doing. She's in Book 1 because she earned her place. The yellow fur and blue eyes make her look innocent. The uniform says otherwise.
