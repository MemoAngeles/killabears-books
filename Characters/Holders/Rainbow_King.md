## Appearance

Rainbow King doesn't reflect light. He refracts it.

His fur shifts through the entire spectrum — not in stripes or patches, but in waves, like oil on water if oil was made of starlight. The Rainbow mutation is rare enough. A bear who learned to weaponize it is something else entirely. The Crown of Containment sits heavy on his head, not decorative — functional. Without it, the prismatic energy would be... uncontained.

He walks and the air around him bends. Colors that shouldn't exist flicker at the edges of his silhouette. It's beautiful. It's also a warning.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Regal bearing — carries himself like royalty because he is
- The crown isn't vanity, it's responsibility
- Sees the world differently — literally, through prismatic eyes
- Protective of his crew with a quiet, absolute authority

**The Sovereign:**
Rainbow King didn't choose the title. The Prismatic Mists chose him. Prolonged dimensional exposure rewrote his fur, his eyes, his perception of reality itself. He sees frequencies other bears can't. He walks between spectrums. The Crown of Containment keeps the worst of it locked down — without it, he'd be a walking dimensional rift.

He leads not by force but by presence. When Rainbow King speaks, the light in the room shifts. People listen.
