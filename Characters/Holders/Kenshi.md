## Appearance

Kenshi is an unsettling presence. Silver-white fur gives him a ghostly appearance. His most striking feature is the white blindfold-like wrapping that covers his snout and lower face—but leaves his eyes visible.

And those eyes... purple with a polka-dot pattern, clearly not natural. The result of experiments that changed him forever.

He wears a purple tie—a strange touch of formality that hints at who he was before.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Grizzled veteran energy
- Dry, dark humor
- Wise but weary
- Appears to be comic relief until the moment he saves everyone

**The Truth:**
Kenshi plays the fool. He makes jokes. He seems harmless.

Then the situation goes sideways, and while everyone else panics, Kenshi calmly solves the problem. He's been through worse. He's SEEN worse—in ways that defy explanation.

---

## Background

### The Experiments

Kenshi was one of Dr. Fogerty Fomo's early experimental subjects—back when Fomo was still being pressured by Bearington to push boundaries. The experiments were meant to enhance perception.

They worked. Too well.

Kenshi's eyes were changed. He can now see things that would melt lesser bears' retinas—Killaverse horrors, dimensional rifts, things that exist between moments. The blindfold isn't for show. It's protection—for everyone else.

### "Real Eyes Realize Real Lies"

His motto. His warning. His curse.

He refuses cybernetic eye replacements on principle. The truth matters more than comfort.

## Role in the Killaverse

Kenshi is the grizzled veteran who's seen it all. Perfect for:
- Dark-humor side character who surprises everyone
- The one who silently saves the squad while everyone panics
- Living connection to Fomo's experimental past
- Warning about what happens when science goes too far

**Lore Significance:**
As one of Fomo's early subjects, Kenshi represents both Fomo's guilt and his genius. The experiments changed Kenshi forever—but also gave him abilities that have saved countless lives.
