## Appearance

Lewie looks like the last bear you'd expect to be dangerous — and that's exactly how he likes it. Checkered fur pattern makes him stand out visually, but the red hoodie and oversized nerdy glasses scream "tech nerd, not threat." He's always got a device in his paws, always tinkering, always three steps ahead in cyberspace while looking two steps behind in real life.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Brilliant with technology, awkward with everything else
- Quiet confidence masked by nerdy demeanor
- Sees the digital layer of everything
- Loyal to the cause through code, not combat

**The Hacker:**
Lewie doesn't kick down doors — he makes doors open themselves. He's the bear who cracks the encryption, spoofs the surveillance, and makes Bearington's tech infrastructure look like a house of cards. Combat? He'd rather not. But give him a terminal and watch empires fall.
