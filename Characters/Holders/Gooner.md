## Appearance

Gooner looks like he's about to fail an exam he didn't know about. Permanently.

Gray fur, neatly groomed — the Kenjōkai Academy doesn't tolerate sloppiness. His uniform is buttoned up tight, teal fabric with gold buttons polished to a mirror shine. Everything about him says "I followed the rules." Everything about his face says "the rules aren't helping."

Those wide eyes don't miss a thing. Not because he's observant by nature, but because he's terrified of what he might miss.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Anxious but competent — panics before, performs during
- By-the-book to a fault, then improvises brilliantly when the book fails
- Overthinks everything, which accidentally makes him thorough
- Loyal to his squad because leaving would mean being alone

**The Cadet:**
Gooner graduated near the top of his Kenjōkai class, and he'll tell you it was luck. It wasn't. He trained harder than anyone because he was convinced everyone else was better. The result: a bear who's technically excellent and emotionally a mess.

In a crisis, something clicks. The worry becomes focus. The overthinking becomes calculation. And then it's over and he goes right back to panicking about the next thing.
