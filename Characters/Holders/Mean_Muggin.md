## Appearance

Mean Muggin earned his name from the face. Beige fur, a black backward cap, and gold grills that flash when he talks. But the eyes — those aren't natural. Robot eyes, called Threadsight Lenses or "Weave-Eyes," replaced whatever he was born with. They glow faintly, scanning, processing, seeing things in the fabric of reality that organic eyes can't perceive.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Tough exterior — the mean mug is permanent
- The Threadsight Lenses changed how he sees everything
- Gold grills are swagger. The robot eyes are power.

**The Threadseer:**
Mean Muggin didn't ask for the Weave-Eyes. But now that he has them, he sees the threads that connect everything — people, events, futures. It's overwhelming. The mean mug? That's what your face looks like when you can see fate unraveling in real time and can't look away.
