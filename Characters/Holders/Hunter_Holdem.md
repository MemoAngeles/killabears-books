## Appearance

Hunter Holdem is camouflage and cards. Green camo fur blends him into any backdrop, but the green casino dealer's visor gives away his true nature — this bear plays games, and the house always wins. His paws move with the practiced grace of someone who's shuffled a million decks and palmed a thousand aces.
