## Appearance

GuruKilla exists in a state of permanent transcendence. Dark brown fur, muscular build (visible arms), but always seated in lotus position. His eyes are hypnotic—yellow with spiral patterns that seem to move if you stare too long.

He wears a pink coat with white fur trim that looks like it belongs on a rap mogul, not a meditation master. Rainbow grills flash when he speaks his wisdom.

The psychedelic mandala that always seems to swirl behind him? Some say it's an illusion. Some say it's real. GuruKilla says "both."

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Profoundly wise
- Speaks in koans and riddles
- Genuinely enlightened (probably)
- Mixes street wisdom with spiritual insight

**The Contradiction:**
GuruKilla looks like he should be dropping mixtapes, not mantras. But his wisdom is undeniable. He's achieved something most bears never will—inner peace in a chaotic universe.

"Y'already know!" is both his catchphrase and his philosophy. The truth is always obvious if you're ready to see it.

## Background

GuruKilla was once a different bear. Ambitious. Hungry. Violent.

Then something happened. He won't say what. Just that he "saw the spiral" and understood everything.

Now he meditates. He teaches. He helps others find what he found.

And occasionally, when absolutely necessary, he reminds everyone why they used to call him "Killa."

---

## Role in the Killaverse

GuruKilla serves as the spiritual anchor—proof that enlightenment is possible even in darkness. Perfect for:
- Dispensing cryptic but useful wisdom
- Calming situations through sheer presence
- The mentor figure who appears when needed
- Demonstrating that strength and peace can coexist
