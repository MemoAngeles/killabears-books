## Appearance

Mr. Bluebear Lennon is a walking contradiction — peace and war, art and violence, wrapped in blue fur and a purple scarf.

The red Lennon glasses give him the look of a philosopher, a dreamer, someone who'd rather write a song than throw a punch. But then there's the black demon half-mask covering the lower half of his face, and suddenly the dreamer looks like something from a nightmare. The purple scarf flows behind him like a banner.

Blue fur, red glasses, black mask, purple scarf — he's a four-color riot of contradictions, and every one of them works.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Artistic soul with a warrior's resolve
- Inspires through voice and presence
- Dual nature — gentle creator, fierce protector
- Believes music is the ultimate weapon

**The War Bard:**
Lennon fights with sound. His battle hymns lift exhausted allies off the ground. His demon mask roar sends enemies scrambling. He didn't choose between art and war — he made them the same thing.
