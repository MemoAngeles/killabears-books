## Appearance

Blu the Brave looks like he stepped out of a legend. Blue fur shines beneath Ursoth-style ancient armor — plates and chainmail forged in a tradition older than anyone alive remembers. The armor is scratched, dented, and beautiful. He wears it like it was made for him. It probably was, a long time ago.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Brave to a fault — the name isn't ironic
- Ancient warrior's soul in a modern conflict
- Honor and duty above all
- The shield between danger and everyone else

**The Brave:**
Blu doesn't flinch. He doesn't calculate odds. He sees a threat, he steps in front of it. The ancient armor isn't just protection — it's a promise. The bears who wore it before him were the same way. Brave to the point of stupidity. Beloved because of it.
