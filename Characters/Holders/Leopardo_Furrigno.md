## Appearance

Leopardo is a cheetah-patterned KillaBear — actual spotted fur, not a shirt. He wears a RED SKI-MASK that covers his face, giving him a permanently menacing look. His build is solid, intimidating. The kind of bear you cross the street to avoid.

The ski-mask is never removed. Nobody knows what's underneath. Nobody asks twice.

## Background

### The Rise of Killa Pizza

Leopardo started young. Delivery boy for mob-owned "laundromats." The work was easy. The pay was good. And Cheeto—as they called him—kept his mouth shut.

Then came the payday. One big score, one quick exit, and Leopardo vanished from that life. Masked up. Changed his identity. Invested everything into a legitimate business.

**Killa Pizza LLC** was born.

Now he runs the most successful pizza chain in Ursamarch. The secret? Great pizza. Absolute loyalty from employees. And the understanding that Leopardo Furrigno handles his own problems.

Leopardo represents the morally grey entrepreneur—not a villain, but not exactly a hero either. Perfect for:

**Potential Story Role:**
Killa Pizza could serve as a front for resistance activities. Leopardo doesn't like Bearington cutting into his business—and he REALLY doesn't like tyranny affecting his customers.
