## Appearance

ZoroBear is a crimson warrior wrapped in steel and tradition.

Red fur blazes beneath a white demon half-mask — the mark of the Kenjōkai order. A bulletproof vest covers his chest, modern protection married to ancient discipline. Two katanas cross his back, always within reach. His stance is always balanced, always ready.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Disciplined and honor-bound
- Silent until it matters
- Follows the Kenjōkai warrior code
- Loyal to those who earn it through action, not words

**The Blade:**
ZoroBear doesn't talk about fighting. He fights. The Kenjōkai code demands precision over fury, and he delivers. Every movement is deliberate. Every strike is earned.
