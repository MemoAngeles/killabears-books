## Appearance

Po Boy is built like a wall that decided to go for a walk. Thick brown fur over a frame that's all density and durability — not the biggest bear, but one of the hardest to put down. The real eye-catcher is the little blue Suzuika perched happily on his head, chirping and bobbing like it's riding a parade float.

The thick hide isn't metaphorical. His fur has an almost leathery quality, layered and tough, like nature decided to give him built-in armor. Scratches and scars dot his frame but nothing deep — everything bounces off Po Boy.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Steady and dependable — the bear you want at your back
- Gentle with his Suzuika companion, fierce with everything else
- Doesn't talk much, lets his presence do the communicating
- Two survivors for the price of one — and he takes that responsibility seriously

**The Bond:**
The Suzuika chose him. Nobody knows why the little blue alien picked this particular bear, but once it climbed up there, it never came down. Po Boy protects the Suzuika. The Suzuika... does something back. The bond runs deeper than either of them can explain.
