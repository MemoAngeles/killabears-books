## Appearance

Ralph is dashing in a reckless, pilot-who-shouldn't-be-alive kind of way. Black fur, aviator goggles pushed up on his forehead or snapped down for action. A blue KillaBears cap sits slightly askew, and a pink scarf trails behind him like a banner. He looks like he just jumped out of something that was on fire — and enjoyed it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Bold and adventurous — lives for the mission
- Pink scarf is his lucky charm (never takes it off)
- Aviator mentality — thinks in altitude and escape routes
- Dependable in a fight, insufferable at a bar

**The Captain:**
Ralph handles the tower assault type missions. The ones where you go up, things explode, and someone has to get everyone out. That someone is Ralph. He's not the strongest, not the smartest, but he's the one who shows up when the building is on fire.
