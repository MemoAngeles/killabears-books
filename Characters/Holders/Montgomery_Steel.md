## Appearance

Montgomery Steel is elegance in bear form. Pure white fur, impeccably maintained. A top hat that somehow never falls off, even in combat. A fitted vest that speaks to a time when bears had *standards*.

But the eyes give him away. That blue-turquoise glow isn't natural — it's the mark of a Blue Suzuika bond, and it pulses brighter when he's using his telekinetic abilities. One moment he's adjusting his hat with a gloved paw; the next, he's hurling an enemy across the room without lifting a finger.

Victorian class. Alien power. Terrifying combination.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Impeccable manners — even in combat
- Calm under pressure to an unsettling degree
- Dry wit sharper than most blades
- Believes violence should be *elegant*

**The Gentleman Fighter:**
Montgomery doesn't brawl. He conducts. Combat is a performance, and he is the maestro. Every telekinetic throw is precise, every deflection graceful. He'll apologize to an enemy before sending them through a wall.

"Terribly sorry. You were in the way."

---

## The Blue Suzuika Bond

Montgomery's glowing blue-turquoise eyes are the visible sign of his bond with a Blue Suzuika — one of the rarer cyclopean alien symbiotes. This bond grants:
- **Telekinesis** — Can move objects and beings with his mind
- **Precision Control** — Not brute force; surgical, exact manipulation
- **Enhanced Perception** — The Suzuika provides spatial awareness
- **The Glow** — Eyes pulse brighter with exertion, a warning to anyone paying attention

The bond suits him perfectly. Why get your paws dirty when you can handle everything from a comfortable distance?

## Role in the Killaverse

The gentleman among berserkers. Montgomery brings class, control, and devastating telekinetic power to any team. He's the calm center of chaos — the one sipping tea (metaphorically) while everyone else is screaming.

**Potential Story Role:**
A refined bear with alien power and old-world values. What drove a Victorian gentleman to bond with an alien symbiote? What does he fight for behind that polished exterior? The hat hides more than just ears.
