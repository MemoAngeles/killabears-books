## Appearance

Dynamo — Mo to his friends, which is everyone because you don't make enemies with the medic — wears his cap backwards like a bear who stopped caring about protocol around the same time he started saving lives. Brown fur, Grimheart bloodline visible in the slightly cooler tint around his extremities, built more for endurance than combat.

His hands are the tell. Steady. Always steady. While everyone else shakes from adrenaline or fear, Mo's hands move with the calm precision of someone who's stitched wounds in the middle of firefights and never missed a stitch.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Calm under pressure — the eye of every storm
- Grimheart bloodline grants natural cold resistance
- Heals first, fights second, complains never
- Exactly who you want nearby when everything's on fire

**The Medic:**
Mo didn't choose to be a medic. The Grimheart bloodline chose for him — cold-resistant, steady-handed, patient by nature. But he's not just a healer with a backwards cap. When the wounded are tended and the threat's still standing, Mo picks up what's available and handles it. Quietly. Competently.
