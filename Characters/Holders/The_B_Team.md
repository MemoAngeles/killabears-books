# The B Team

---

## Overview

**The B Team** is a legendary four-bear squad operating in the Killaverse—soldiers of fortune who help the helpless, punish the wicked, and look absolutely ICONIC doing it.

Inspired by a certain classic TV show, The B Team combines military precision, chaotic energy, and unshakeable brotherhood.

*"If you have a problem, if no one else can help, and if you can find them... maybe you can hire The B Team."*

## Background

The B Team were once elite soldiers—the best of the best. Then they were framed for a crime they didn't commit. They escaped to the Ursamarch underground, where they survive as soldiers of fortune.

They help those who can't help themselves. They take on jobs no one else will. And they NEVER leave a bear behind.

---

## Combat Approach

1. **Hannibear** makes the plan
2. **Furington** gets them inside
3. **Murdock** provides aerial support (if needed) or chaos (always needed)
4. **B.A.** handles anyone who needs handling
5. The plan somehow works despite everything going wrong

---

## Role in the Killaverse

The B Team represents found family and heroism-for-hire. Perfect for:
- Mercenary assistance for the resistance
- Heist/mission episodes
- Comic relief through team dynamics
- Proof that misfits can be heroes
