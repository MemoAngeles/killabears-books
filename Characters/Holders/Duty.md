## Appearance

Duty looks like the bear who has everything handled. Because he does.

Dark brown fur, rich and well-kept — not flashy, just solid. Round eyes that don't miss details but never look stressed about them. The white guayabera is always pressed, always clean, even in a crisis. That alone should tell you everything about who Duty is.

No armor. No weapons strapped to his back. Just a bear in a nice shirt who happens to know where every supply crate, medical kit, and escape route is on the entire ship.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Calm under pressure — the calmer things get, the worse the situation probably is
- Keeps inventory of everything, including people
- The bear everyone goes to when things break
- Never raises his voice. Never needs to.

**The Quartermaster:**
Every crew needs someone who actually makes things work. Not the captain, not the hero — the bear who makes sure the hero has ammunition and the captain has coffee. Duty doesn't seek glory. He seeks efficiency. And somehow, when the dust settles, everyone realizes Duty was the reason they survived.

He showed up in a guayabera. He kept the guayabera clean through a ship crash. That's the kind of bear he is.
