## Appearance

Dizzy's eyes are the first thing you notice and the last thing you want to see. Twin spirals spinning endlessly inward, hypnotic and wrong, like staring into a drain that leads somewhere you don't want to go. Ancestral brown fur — the old bloodline, the deep roots — covers a compact brawler's frame built for chaos, not elegance.

Those fangs aren't for show. Sharp, prominent, always visible because Dizzy's mouth is always set in something between a grin and a snarl. The spiral eyes aren't a disability. They're a weapon nobody sees coming.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Thrives in disorder — the more chaotic things get, the sharper he becomes
- ROOT-immune — whatever the ROOT corruption does to other bears, it slides off Dizzy
- Unpredictable and disorienting to allies and enemies alike
- The spiral eyes aren't a weakness. They're a warning.

**The Chaos:**
Most bears fight despite being disoriented. Dizzy fights *because* of it. The constant spiral vision has rewired his brain — he processes chaos faster than order. When the battlefield falls apart, Dizzy's just getting started.
