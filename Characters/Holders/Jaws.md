## Appearance

Jaws is the nightmare you don't wake up from.

Cheetah-patterned fur — not painted, not dyed, born that way — covers a frame built for violence. But the fur isn't what makes people run. It's the skull. The Ceta Ko curse has literally split his face open, a bone-white skull pushing through flesh and fur as if something inside him is trying to get out. Maybe it already has.

His claws aren't claws anymore — they're bone, hardened and sharpened by the curse into weapons that can shred through armor. The leopard hide is natural, a genetic wildcard that makes him look like a predator wearing a predator's skin.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Feral and barely contained
- The curse speaks to him — he doesn't always disagree with it
- Protective of allies in his own terrifying way
- More animal than bear, more skull than animal

**The Cursed:**
The Ceta Ko curse didn't break Jaws. It made him something else. He walks the razor edge between bear and beast, between controlled fury and total berserker abandon. His allies trust him because he points himself at enemies. His enemies... don't trust anything anymore.
