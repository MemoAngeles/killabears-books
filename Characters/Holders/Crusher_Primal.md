## Appearance

Crusher (Primal) is the ancient version of the modern berserker — rawer, wilder, closer to the source. Black fur matted and thick, draped in caveman pelts from beasts he killed with bare paws. Ice grills gleam — frozen trophies in his jaw. Those trademark dizzy spiral eyes are the same across timelines: the mark of a bear who's taken too many hits and keeps swinging.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Tribal chief — leads through raw dominance
- Primal-era bear with no modern filter
- Chief of the Tarlands tribe near Cinder Gulf
- Same Crusher rage, ancient packaging

**The Chief:**
In the Primal era, leadership isn't elected — it's earned through survival and violence. Crusher earned the Tarlands chieftainship by being the last bear standing, every time. The ice grills are his crown. The caveman pelt is his throne room. Cinder Gulf is his kingdom.
