## Appearance

Merric is a cub who wears more power than most full-grown bears can handle. Camo fur hides beneath skull armor. Demon wings spread from his back. A demon tail curls behind him. A royal crown sits on his head — earned or stolen, nobody asks twice. And in his paw, a cursed flaming sword that mirrors Kaenix's own burden. He is small, armored, winged, tailed, crowned, and very, very dangerous.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Regal bearing in a cub's body — the crown isn't decorative
- Demon features grant him unsettling authority
- The cursed sword is a choice, not an accident
- More dangerous than any three adult bears combined

**The Prince:**
Merric claims royalty and nobody disputes it. The crown, the wings, the tail — it all adds up to something that shouldn't exist. A demon prince in cub form, wielding a weapon of fire and wearing armor of bone. Whether he's a blessing or a curse depends on whose side he's on.
