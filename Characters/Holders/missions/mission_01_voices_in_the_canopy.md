## POST 2 — THE JOURNEY (Day 1, Evening or Day 2)

**🖼️ IMAGE: "The Verdant Spine — Deep Jungle"**
Dense tropical jungle with impossibly tall trees, bioluminescent fungi on trunks, thick vines hanging like curtains. Shafts of green-gold light cutting through the canopy. In the distance, faint Thread energy visible as shimmering golden-blue wisps tangled in the branches. Something is wrong with them — they're knotted, pulsing irregularly. Mood: beautiful but unsettling.

**Tweet:**

🌿 MISSION LOG — THE VANGUARD — HOUR 3

The jungle swallowed them within minutes of leaving camp.

Honey Bear moves 50 meters ahead. Silent. Fast. She spots it first — claw marks on the trees. Deep. Recent. Something big passed through here, heading the same direction they are.

Rainbow King raises a paw. His prismatic aura flickers, colors shifting wrong.

*"The Thread here is... tangled. Like something knotted it on purpose."*

Kitsune's ears flatten. Jaws growls low — the skull is pushing forward. He smells something that isn't animal, isn't plant. Something old.

Blu the Brave plants his shield. *"Movement. Up in the canopy."*

They freeze. The jungle is watching.

Three options:

🅰️ PURSUE — Follow the claw marks into the deep jungle
🅱️ CLIMB — Get above the canopy for a better view
🅲️ HOLD — Defensive formation. Let whatever's watching come to them

Squad — what's the call? 👇

## POST 4 — THE REVELATION (Day 3)

**🖼️ IMAGE: "The Knotted Thread Nexus"**
A sacred clearing in the jungle. At the center, a massive ancient tree with Thread energy visibly flowing through it like glowing veins — but the threads are knotted, tangled, some blackened. Lone Paws totems surround the tree. Small tribal fires. The feeling that this place is ancient and holy and something is WRONG with it. Mood: sacred, broken, urgent.

**Tweet:**

🌿 MISSION LOG — THE VANGUARD — THE NEXUS

Rok leads them to a place his people call the Threadheart — an ancient tree where the Thread energy of the Verdant Spine converges.

It's dying.

The golden veins that once flowed through the tree are knotted. Blackened. Some have stopped pulsing entirely. The air hums wrong.

Rok removes his sunglasses for the first time. His eyes glow faintly — Thread-touched.

*"This started before you fell from the sky. But your crash made it worse. The Thread is tangled where your ship bleeds metal into the earth."*

He looks at Rainbow King. *"You feel it. Your colors are the Thread made visible."*

He looks at Jaws. *"And you — the Ceta Ko feeds on knotted Thread. That's why your skull is pushing harder here."*

Jaws snarls. The skull recedes — barely.

*"The Maw is beneath this tree. It was sealed long before my grandfather's grandfather. Something is trying to unseal it."*

Rok puts the sunglasses back on.

*"I need to know — will your people help us seal it again? Or will you take what's inside for yourselves?"*

🅰️ ALLIANCE — "We'll help. Whatever's down there stays sealed."
🅱️ INVESTIGATE — "We need to know what the Maw IS before we promise anything."
🅲️ BARGAIN — "We'll help — but our ship needs repairs. We need resources."

Squad — and @Digital_dad23 — what happens next? 👇

## POST 5 — THE RESOLUTION (Day 3 or 4)

**🖼️ IMAGE: "First Alliance"**
Two groups of bears standing together at the Threadheart tree. On one side: the Vanguard squad in their Fomo-era gear. On the other: Rok's Lone Paws warriors in tribal paint and bone armor. Between them, a small fire — the Thread energy around it slightly healthier, brighter. The beginning of something. Mood: cautious hope, two worlds meeting.

**Tweet (adapt based on choice):**

**If A (Alliance):**

🌿 MISSION LOG — THE VANGUARD — FIRST ALLIANCE

Rainbow King steps forward. His prismatic aura flares — and for a moment, the knotted Thread energy around the tree loosens. Just slightly. Enough for everyone to feel it.

*"We didn't choose to crash here. But we're here. And whatever the Maw is — sealed is better than open."*

Rok nods. The first nod of respect between the fallen sky-bears and the Lone Paws.

Honey Bear offers Rok's scouts a ration bar from the ship. They sniff it suspiciously. One bites. His eyes widen.

Jaws and a Lone Paws warrior lock eyes. Neither blinks. Neither attacks. Progress.

Blu the Brave plants his shield at the base of the Threadheart. *"We hold this line."*

Kitsune bows — Kenjōkai style. Rok tilts his head. He's seen that bow before. In his visions.

**If B (Investigate):**

Rainbow King: *"We don't make promises blind. Show us the Maw. Then we decide."*

Rok smiles behind the mask. *"Smart. The last chief who promised blind didn't survive it."*

He leads them to the base of the tree. Beneath the roots — a sealed stone door, covered in Thread-script that pulses faintly. Even Jaws steps back.

*"This is as far as we go today. But now you've seen it. Now you dream about it. That's how the Maw works."*

**If C (Bargain):**

Kitsune: *"Our ship is broken. People are hurt. We help you — you help us."*

Rok laughs. First time they've heard him laugh.

*"A trade? In my jungle? You learn fast, sky-bear."*

*"Timber. Vine-rope. Healer herbs. We have these. You have metal. Tools. Whatever those 'ration bars' are. My scouts won't stop eating them."*
