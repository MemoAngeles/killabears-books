## Appearance

Krusty is what happens when a KillaCub watches too many horror movies — and decides to become one.

Purple fur barely visible beneath a porcelain clown mask that grins when Krusty isn't. Sharp fangs poke out from under the mask's painted smile. One paw clutches a red balloon that bobs unnervingly in still air. Purple boots complete the look — a tiny terror clown who'd be adorable if he weren't so deeply unsettling.

The balloon never pops. Nobody knows why. Nobody wants to find out.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Creepy-cute — oscillates between adorable cub and nightmare fuel
- Uses fear as a weapon because he's too small for brute force
- Surprisingly clever and manipulative in combat
- The balloon is... important. Don't touch the balloon.

**The Terror Clown:**
Krusty figured out early that a KillaCub can't outfight OG KillaBears. But he CAN out-creep them. The mask, the balloon, the fangs — it's all theater, and the battlefield is his stage. Enemies who underestimate him because of his size learn quickly that size doesn't matter when you're too scared to swing.
