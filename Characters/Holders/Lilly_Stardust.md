## Appearance

Lilly is a blue-furred KillaCub with unmistakable anime-inspired starry eyes — golden, star-shaped pupils that shimmer like they're reflecting entire galaxies. Her blue ponytail sweeps back into a distinctive circular hair loop, and a small bandage on her forehead hints at past adventures (or clumsiness — she'll never tell).

She wears orange and purple striped socks pulled high with clean white sneakers, giving her a playful, youthful look that contrasts sharply with the cosmic weight behind those golden eyes.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Perceptive beyond her years
- Quiet but observant — misses nothing
- Cosmic intuition guides her instincts
- Gentle demeanor hides sharp insight

**The Stargazer:**
Lilly doesn't fight like the others. She *sees* like no one else can. Her starry eyes aren't just for show — they grant her a form of cosmic perception that lets her notice what others overlook: hidden passages, concealed motives, things lurking just outside normal sight.

She's the one who tugs on a warrior's sleeve and whispers, "Don't go that way."

They've learned to listen.

## Role in the Killaverse

Every squad needs eyes, and Lilly's see further than most. She's the early warning system, the one who spots the trap before it closes. Small, quiet, easily underestimated — which is exactly how she likes it.

**Potential Story Role:**
A young cub whose cosmic perception makes her invaluable to any team. She doesn't seek glory — she seeks truth. And those golden star-shaped pupils have a way of finding it, whether the truth wants to be found or not.
