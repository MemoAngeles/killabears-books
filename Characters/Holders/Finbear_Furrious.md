## Appearance

Green fur marks him immediately — and the military bearing confirms it. Finbear Furrious carries himself like a bear who's given orders that got people killed and made peace with it. Every movement is deliberate, controlled, efficient.

He doesn't dress for show. His gear is functional, worn, and maintained with obsessive care. The kind of bear who cleans his equipment before he cleans himself.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Born leader — tactical, decisive, unwavering
- Military discipline runs bone-deep
- Carries the weight of command without complaint
- Inspires loyalty through competence, not charisma

**The Commander:**
Finbear doesn't give speeches. He gives orders — clear, precise, and right. The bears under his command don't follow him because he's loud or inspiring. They follow him because he's *correct*. His plans work. His calls save lives.

When Finbear says move, you move. When he says hold, you hold. When he says run — you've never seen him say run.

## Role in the Killaverse

**Canon presence in Book 1: TIMEFALL.** Finbear Furrious is the military backbone of the rebel forces. Where others bring individual power, he brings *organization* — turning a collection of fighters into an army.

He also holds a **Primal Kard**, connecting him to ancient power that predates the modern Killaverse.

**Story Role:**
The commander who holds the line. The strategic mind behind the resistance's military operations. His friendship with Oisín Ogma (both from Cloverlands) adds personal stakes to the larger war.
