## Appearance

Icubus is unsettling in the way only cubs can be — small, dark, and wrong in all the right ways. Black fur absorbs light. Golden wings sprout from his back, too ornate for something so young. Red glasses frame cross-stitch eyes that seem sewn shut but see everything. The gold pacifier in his mouth glints like a warning: *don't let the baby fool you.*

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Eerily calm for a cub
- Sees more than he should
- The gold pacifier is a comfort — and a focus
- Silent observer who acts decisively when it matters

**The Omen:**
Other cubs play. Icubus watches. There's something ancient behind those cross-stitch eyes, something that arrived with the golden wings. He doesn't cry. He doesn't fuss. He just... knows things.
