## Appearance

Lady P is what happens when beauty becomes a weapon.

Red-pink fur, flawless. Dark blue-black hair cut in sharp bangs that frame her face like a warning. Green eyes that don't blink when they should. She dresses professionally—cream blouses, tailored pieces—because looking good while being terrifying is the point.

When she looks at you from a car window on a rainy night, you know your life is about to change. Probably not for the better.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Ice cold under pressure
- Never bluffs—ever
- Precision over spectacle
- Few words, maximum impact

**The Lady P Effect:**
Lady P doesn't threaten. She doesn't need to. She states facts. "You have until tomorrow" isn't a warning. It's a countdown. Everyone who's ever tested her deadlines learned exactly once.

---

## Background

### Origins

Lady P's history before The Syndicate is murky—intentionally so. Some say she's $terlin's niece by blood. Others say she earned her place through sheer competence. The truth doesn't matter. What matters is that $terlin trusts her with the things that require certainty.

### Rise to Power

She started as an enforcer. Not the loud kind—the quiet kind. The kind who shows up, explains the situation once, and leaves. Problems stopped being problems.

$terlin noticed. Now she handles the operations that can't fail.
