## Appearance

Zed is red silence. Crimson fur beneath a leather mask, and where the mouth should be — a zipper. Sealed shut. Whether by choice, curse, or surgery, Zed doesn't speak. Ever. The leather mask covers most of his face, leaving only intense eyes that communicate everything words would fumble.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Completely silent — the zipper mouth isn't decorative
- Communicates through action, gesture, and presence
- Red fur suggests fire inside; the silence contains it
- The most unsettling quiet in any room

**The Silent:**
Zed's silence isn't peaceful. It's loaded. Every bear who's been near him describes the same thing: the feeling that he's about to say something devastating, if only the zipper would open. It doesn't. Maybe it can't. Maybe he won't let it.
