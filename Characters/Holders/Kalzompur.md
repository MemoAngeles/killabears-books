## Appearance

Kalzompur is what happens when death decides it isn't finished with you yet.

Purple-furred and rotting, this X3 Mutant walks the line between undead horror and unstoppable force. One eye glows a furious red — the other is... well, best not to look too closely. His ears are torn and chewed, whether by enemies or by himself in fits of rage, nobody's sure. Stuffing pokes out from wounds that never heal and never need to.

He doesn't bleed. He seeps.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Driven by primal rage
- Surprisingly resilient — refuses to stay down
- Unpredictable in combat and conversation
- Speaks in short, guttural bursts when he speaks at all

**The Berserker:**
Kalzompur doesn't strategize. Kalzompur doesn't negotiate. Kalzompur arrives, and things break. The mutation that brought him back from the dead left his tactical mind intact but buried it under layers of feral instinct. When the rage takes over, allies know to clear a path.
