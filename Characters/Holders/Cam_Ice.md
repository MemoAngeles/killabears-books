## Appearance

You don't see Cam Ice until he wants you to. And by then, the ice grills are the last thing you'll remember.

Green camo fur blends seamlessly into any environment — jungle, urban, rift-touched wasteland. A battered safari hat sits atop his head, practical rather than fashionable. One eye is hidden behind a cyborg visor that feeds tactical data directly into his brain. And when he opens his mouth? Ice grills. Gleaming, frozen, intimidating.

He looks like a nature documentary host who moonlights as a spec-ops assassin. Which... isn't far off.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Patient and observational — watches before acting
- Tech-enhanced but instinct-driven
- Cool demeanor that matches the ice in his name
- Professional, efficient, no wasted movements

**The Operative:**
Cam Ice is the bear you send ahead. The one who maps the territory, identifies the threats, marks the targets, and sometimes handles them before the team even arrives. His cyborg enhancements complement natural recon instincts — part predator, part machine, fully effective.
