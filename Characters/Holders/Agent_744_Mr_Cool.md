## Appearance

Agent 744 is built like a covert ops poster boy — gray fur, stitched scars that tell stories he'll never share, and those ever-present dark sunglasses that haven't left his face since the day he earned the codename "Mr. Cool."

Everything about him screams action hero: the deliberate stance, the calm breathing, the way he scans a room before anyone else even notices the door opened. The stitches across his face aren't cosmetic — they're souvenirs from missions that would've killed lesser bears.

His agent badge glints under his collar. He doesn't flash it. He doesn't need to.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Ice cold under pressure
- Calculated and methodical
- Loyal to the mission above all else
- Dry, cutting humor — when he bothers to speak

**The Operative:**
Agent 744 doesn't do small talk. He does objectives. Every word is measured, every movement intentional. He earned "Mr. Cool" not because he's stylish (though he is), but because nothing — absolutely nothing — rattles him.

Behind the sunglasses, there's a bear who's seen too much and processed all of it into something useful: competence.
