## Appearance

Raw-B is what happens when mutation goes RIGHT—in the worst possible way for his enemies.

Rare green fur that seems to absorb light. Small, glowing eyes that see through everything. And that Black Demon mask—covering just the lower half of his face, revealing sharp teeth in a grin that never quite reaches "friendly."

He wears what the records call "Evil clothing"—dark, utilitarian, designed for someone who doesn't need to be seen until it's too late.

Raw-B doesn't hunt for chaos. He FILTERS it. He moves through the noise of factions and false power like water through a sieve, and what remains after he's passed... is truth.

He doesn't threaten. He doesn't posture. He just appears, does what needs doing, and leaves. By the time you notice him, the damage is already done.

## Background

### Origin: A Rogue Construct

Raw-B was born from the early Killaverse mutations—the experimental phase when Bearington's scientists were still figuring out what worked. Most of those early subjects died. Some went mad. A few became weapons.

Raw-B became something else: **too raw to be controlled.**

He walked out of the facility. Nobody stopped him. Nobody COULD stop him. And he's been operating on his own terms ever since.

### The Breaker of Illusions

In the Killaverse, Raw-B has earned a specific reputation: he breaks illusions. False power. False alliances. False strength. When Raw-B gets involved, pretense dies.

*"His presence is often felt long before it's seen. And by the time he's noticed, the damage is already done."*

## Role in the Killaverse

Raw-B operates outside the normal power structures:

- Not aligned with the Resistance

He's the variable nobody can account for. When Raw-B appears, all calculations change.

**Story Function:**
Raw-B could be the one who exposes hidden truths—Bearington's secrets, Tachyon's manipulations, the lies that hold the current order together. He doesn't take sides. He just reveals what IS.
