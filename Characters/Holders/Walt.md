## Appearance

Walt is a pink bear who looks like he's been screaming internally for years and it finally leaked onto his face.

Bright bubblegum pink fur. Wide, hollow eyes that have SEEN things. Mouth permanently open in an expression of "are you KIDDING me right now?" The look of a bear who got dragged into something stupid and knows EXACTLY how this is going to end.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Sarcastic to his core
- Perpetually skeptical
- The voice of reason nobody listens to
- Nervous energy masked by deadpan delivery

**The David Spade Energy:**
Walt is the guy standing next to the chaos, arms crossed, muttering "this is a bad idea" while everyone else charges forward. He's the one who sees the obvious flaw in the plan. He's the one who says "I told you so" when it all goes wrong.

But here's the thing: he's always there. Complaining the whole time, but THERE.

---

## Philosophy

Walt's worldview:
- "This is going to end badly."
- "Did anyone think this through? Anyone? No? Cool cool cool."
- "I'm not saying I'm smarter than everyone, but... actually, yes I am."
- "Why am I here? Seriously. Why am I always HERE?"

---

## The Skeptic's Role

Every group needs a Walt. Someone who:
- Points out the obvious problems
- Provides sarcastic commentary
- Gets dragged along despite protests
- Somehow survives through sheer pessimistic preparation

**Classic Walt Moment:**
Everyone's celebrating a plan. Walt's in the corner: "So we're just NOT going to talk about the seventeen ways this could kill us? Okay. That's fine. I'll just be over here. Writing my will."

## Role in the Killaverse

Walt is the reluctant participant—the everyman who got sucked into adventure and is NOT happy about it. Perfect for:
- Comic relief through constant complaints
- The one who asks the questions the audience is thinking
- Voice of reason in a cast of maniacs
- Surprisingly clutch when it matters

**Story Function:**
Walt grounds the chaos. When everyone else is being heroic, Walt reminds us that normal bears would be TERRIFIED right now. And then he does the heroic thing anyway, while complaining about it.

## The Tommy Boy Dynamic

Walt works best paired with someone who's the opposite—a big, enthusiastic, oblivious optimist who drags him into situations. Walt provides the commentary. The optimist provides the chaos. Together, they somehow survive.

**Ideal Partner Types:**
- Knox (chaos vs. skepticism)
- Bodhi (good vibes vs. bad expectations)
- Kino Da Koala (manic vs. deadpan)
