## Appearance

Krueger is nightmare fuel given fur.

Black fur almost invisible in darkness. Grey-white hair swept dramatically to one side. And that MASK—a white hockey mask splattered with red, covering his face, with only hollow black eyes visible through the holes.

His nose on the mask is a yellow cheese wedge—an absurd touch that somehow makes him MORE unsettling, not less.

## Background

### The Ultimate Nightmare

Where did Krueger come from? Nobody knows for certain. Some say he was an ordinary bear who snapped. Some say he emerged from the collective nightmares of Ursamarch. Some say he was always there, waiting in the dark.

What everyone agrees on: when Krueger KillaClaw appears, bad things happen to bad people.

He's not random in his violence. He has a code—twisted, yes, but consistent. He targets those who deserve it. The corrupt. The cruel. The ones who think they're untouchable.

They're not.
