## Appearance

Black fur. Total black — the kind of black that doesn't reflect light so much as eat it. Mog moves through shadows like he was born there, which, given the cat eyes, might not be far from the truth. Those eyes are the giveaway: vertical slit pupils that glow faintly in darkness, seeing everything while revealing nothing.

The rainbow shades are cosmetic. He knows it. Everyone knows it. He wears them anyway because Mog doesn't do stealth without style. And that grin — always present, always knowing — says he already mapped every exit before you finished your sentence.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Feline instincts in a bear's body — fast, silent, unpredictable
- Always knows the way out before he walks in
- The grin is permanent and deeply unsettling to enemies
- Style is non-negotiable, even in combat

**The Stalker:**
Mog doesn't fight fair. He doesn't fight loud. He appears behind you, already grinning, and by the time you turn around the second time, he's somewhere else entirely. The cat eyes aren't just for seeing in the dark — they're for seeing everything you thought was hidden.
