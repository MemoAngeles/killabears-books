## Appearance

Shirokiba — "White Fang" — wears his name in his colors. Panda fur in stark black and white, bisected by a white demon half-mask that covers the upper face. A tactical vest over his chest speaks to practical warrior sensibility. He is the Kenjōkai order's purest expression: honor, duty, and a blade that serves both.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Honor above everything — the code is not optional
- "White Fang" — his bite is as sharp as his blade
- The most traditional Kenjōkai warrior
- Judges others by their actions, not their words

**The Honor Blade:**
Where ZoroBear is discipline and Kamikaze Kitsune is chaos, Shirokiba is *honor*. He is the Kenjōkai ideal. Every action weighed, every strike justified, every fallen enemy acknowledged. He doesn't enjoy fighting. He accepts it as necessary.
