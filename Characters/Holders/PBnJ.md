## Appearance

PBnJ doesn't walk into a room. The room notices PBnJ was already there.

Black fur that drinks in the light around it, making the edges of his silhouette hard to pin down. And then the eyes — blank, white, glowing faintly like two moons trapped in a skull. No pupils. No expression. Just light where there shouldn't be any.

The pink feather robe is the contradiction. Luxurious, flamboyant, draped across dark fur like a sunset bleeding into a void. It moves when there's no wind. It's the kind of outfit that says "look at me" worn by a bear that says "you can't."

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Speaks rarely, and always in statements that sound like riddles
- The glamour is the mask — what's underneath is the weapon
- Comfortable in darkness, literally and figuratively
- Trusts no one fully, protects everyone quietly

**The Mystic:**
Nobody knows where PBnJ learned what he knows. The blank eyes suggest something happened — a ritual, an accident, a bargain. He doesn't explain. The pink feathers suggest he doesn't care what you think about it. He's the bear who sits in the corner of the ship saying nothing for hours, then drops one sentence that saves the mission.
