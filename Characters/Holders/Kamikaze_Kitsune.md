## Appearance

Kamikaze Kitsune is color and danger wearing a fox's face. Multicolored fur explodes beneath a sleek black turtleneck. The Kitsune fox mask — white with red markings — hides his identity and channels the trickster spirit of the fox. He's Kenjōkai, but where ZoroBear is discipline and Shirokiba is honor, Kamikaze is chaos with a warrior's training.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Trickster warrior — Kenjōkai trained but fox-spirited
- "Kamikaze" isn't a metaphor — he takes suicidal risks
- The fox mask grants cunning the bear code wouldn't allow
- Colorful, chaotic, terrifyingly effective

**The Kitsune:**
The Kenjōkai order values discipline. Kamikaze Kitsune values results. He trained with the same masters as ZoroBear, learned the same forms, took the same oaths — then put on a fox mask and decided rules were more like suggestions. He's their best and worst student simultaneously.
