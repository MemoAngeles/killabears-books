## Appearance

Lil Chav looks like he just stole something. He probably just stole something.

Bright yellow fur that makes stealth impossible, which doesn't stop him from trying. Purple KillaBears cap tilted at maximum attitude angle, a band-aid across his nose from the last fight (or the one before that — there's always a fight). Red clown nose that he was born with and absolutely weaponizes for distraction. Tongue permanently out because respect is for bears who can catch him.

Gray t-shirt, golden boots laced up loose because tying them tight is for nerds. He sits like he owns wherever he's sitting. He doesn't. He doesn't own anything. That's never stopped him.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Mouthy, fast, and completely unafraid of consequences
- Gets into trouble faster than he gets out of it (and he gets out fast)
- Loyal to his crew in his own chaotic way
- The band-aid is never in the same place twice

**The Street Kid:**
Lil Chav didn't grow up in the nice part of the ship. He grew up in whatever part he happened to be running through. No training, no academy, no formal anything — just reflexes, attitude, and golden boots that have kicked more shins than anyone's comfortable admitting.

He's the cub the other cubs look up to and the adults keep an eye on.
