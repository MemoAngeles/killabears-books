# The Syndicate

---

## Overview

**The Syndicate** is a family-run criminal organization operating in the shadows of Ursamarch. Part mob family, part business empire, all dangerous.

While Bearington controls the city from his tower, The Syndicate controls everything he doesn't see—the underground markets, the smuggling routes, the information networks, and the bears who've fallen through the cracks of "legitimate" society.

## Philosophy

**"Bearington thinks he owns this city. He owns the buildings. We own the streets."**

The Syndicate operates on three principles:
1. **Family first** - Blood is thicker than Bearington Coin
2. **Respect is currency** - More valuable than money
3. **The old ways work** - Loyalty, territory, and consequences

---

## Relationship to Main Story

The Syndicate exists in the gray area between Bearington's regime and the Resistance:

- **Not allies of Bearington** - They predate his rule and resent his control
- **Not allies of the Resistance** - They don't believe in causes, just business
- **Potential allies of opportunity** - The enemy of my enemy...

**Story Hook:** The Syndicate has information networks the Resistance needs. The Resistance has access the Syndicate wants. An uneasy alliance could form—or a dangerous rivalry.

---

## Territory

The Syndicate controls:
- **Lowtown black markets** - Where you get what you can't buy legally
- **Port March smuggling** - Import/export outside Bearington's tariffs
- **The Underlevels gambling** - High stakes, higher risks
- **Information brokering** - Knowing things is their real business
