## Appearance

Larry looks like he just woke up. He always looks like he just woke up.

Beige fur, unremarkable build, the kind of bear you'd walk past without a second glance — except for those eyes. Blank white, half-lidded, staring at something that isn't there. Or maybe staring at everything that is. Hard to tell with Larry.

He moves slowly, deliberately, like a bear walking through water that nobody else can see. There's no urgency in him. Even when things are exploding. *Especially* when things are exploding.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Unfazed by literally everything
- Says very little, but when he talks, it lands
- Appears lazy — actually perceiving on a different frequency
- Accidentally wise

**The Dreamer:**
Nobody knows if Larry is incredibly deep or incredibly checked out. He drifts through chaos like he's watching it on a screen. Other bears panic, strategize, argue — Larry just... stands there. And then says one sentence that changes the entire plan.

The blank eyes aren't empty. They're full of something nobody else can access yet.
