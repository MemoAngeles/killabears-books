## Appearance

UNrealZ is what happens when death doesn't take. Purple zombie fur barely clings to a frame that shouldn't still be moving. A bone juts through his skull like a crown he never asked for. His ribs are exposed — not a wound, a statement. His jaw hangs unhinged, capable of unholy roars that shake the walls.

**Core Traits:**
