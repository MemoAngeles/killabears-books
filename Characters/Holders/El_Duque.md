## Appearance

El Duque is a gunslinger who wears his heart in his eyes — literally. Black fur, a weathered cowboy hat, and a bulletproof vest speak to a life of violence. The bandana covers his lower face. But those heart eyes? They're real. Somewhere under the cowboy exterior, the Duke has feelings. He just shoots anyone who mentions it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Strong, silent gunslinger type
- The heart eyes betray a romantic soul
- Cowboy code — honor among outlaws
- The most serious of ScooterMcScoob's bears

**The Duke:**
El Duque rides alone. The cowboy hat, the vest, the bandana — it's not a costume, it's a creed. He believes in justice served hot and personal. The heart eyes are the only soft thing about him, and he'll put a bullet next to anyone who laughs about them.
