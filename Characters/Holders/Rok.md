## Appearance

Rok is a collision of timelines. Blue-striped fur marks him as Primal — ancient, tribal, raw. But the sunglasses? Those aren't from any era a Primal should know. They're from the future, and no one knows how he got them. A red demon half-mask covers half his face, Kenjōkai-style but worn with Primal authority. He stands like a chief. He looks like a paradox.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Natural leader — Chief of the Lone Paws tribe
- Carries knowledge he shouldn't have (the sunglasses)
- Primal strength with mysterious future awareness
- Book 2's central character — his story changes everything

**The Chief:**
Rok leads the Lone Paws — a tribe of Primals who answer to no one. But Rok is different from other chiefs. The sunglasses grant him visions. The mask grants him fury. Together, they make him a leader who can see threats before they arrive and crush them when they do.
