## Appearance

Kushy is unmistakably chill. Deep red fur, a red backwards baseball cap, and X-eyes that look permanently relaxed. He wears a simple dark outfit and always seems to be in a perpetual state of calm.

His posture screams "no stress"—shoulders relaxed, movements slow and deliberate, like he's moving through honey and enjoying every second of it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Extremely laid-back
- Surprisingly wise when he bothers to speak
- Never panics—about anything
- Moves at his own pace, refuses to be rushed

**The Hidden Depth:**
Kushy's relaxed demeanor isn't laziness—it's perspective. He's figured out that most things people stress about don't matter. The things that DO matter? He handles those with surprising competence.

"You ever notice how the chill guy is always the one who survives?"

## Philosophy

Kushy lives by a simple code:

- Move slow, think fast
- Good vibes attract good outcomes
- The universe provides if you let it
