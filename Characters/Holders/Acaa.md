## Appearance

Acaa looks like he's about to tell you something you don't want to hear. He usually is.

Gray fur, muted and professional, the kind of bear who blends into the background until you need him — and then he's the most important bear in the room. Brown vest over a cream shirt, practical and clean. No lab coat. Lab coats are for bears who work in labs. Acaa works in the field, in the wreckage, in the chaos.

Those blank glowing eyes are the unsettling part. They scan you. Not your face — your everything. Acaa looks at a bear and sees blood pressure, fractures, internal bleeding. He doesn't need equipment. The eyes do the work.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Clinical calm — nothing shakes him, he's seen worse
- Blunt honesty — sugarcoating wastes time that patients don't have
- Those eyes never stop scanning, even off duty
- Secretly cares deeply, hides it behind professionalism

**El Doctor:**
Nobody calls him Acaa in the field. He's El Doctor. The bear you carry your wounded to. The bear who looks at a hopeless situation and says "I've seen worse" and means it. His blank eyes aren't empty — they're full of every patient he's ever saved and every one he hasn't. He remembers all of them.
