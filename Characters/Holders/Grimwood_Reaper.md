## Appearance

Grimwood Reaper is a nightmare carved from wood. Wooden fur — or is it bark? — covers a small cub frame. A wooden mask with hollow eyes hides whatever face lies beneath. He carries a scythe sized for a cub but sharp enough for anything. He moves like a puppet with invisible strings, jerky and wrong, and the sound of creaking wood follows him everywhere.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Silent. The puppet doesn't speak.
- Moves with puppet-like jerky animation
- A nightmare given wooden form
- Nobody knows who holds his strings

**The Puppet:**
Grimwood Reaper wasn't born — he was carved. Or cursed. Or dreamed into existence by something that shouldn't dream. He follows no one's orders that anyone can see, but he acts with purpose. The scythe swings. The mask stares. The wood creaks.
