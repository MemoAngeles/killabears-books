## Appearance

Grills-bear looks like a living plush toy that's been loved too hard and stitched back together. His beige-colored fur is held together by visible white stitches across his body—arms, legs, torso, everywhere. His eyes are red buttons with white X stitching, giving him a perpetually quirky expression.

But his most distinctive feature? That SMILE. Rainbow grills flash when he grins—red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple teeth that catch the light like a disco ball made of dental work.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Unfailingly supportive and loyal
- Always brings a smile to others
- Surprisingly perceptive despite his goofy appearance
- "Can see your soul" - reads people with unsettling accuracy

**The Paradox:**
Don't let the button eyes fool you. Donnie has an uncanny ability to see through people—their lies, their fears, their true intentions. The button eyes might not see the physical world clearly, but they see something deeper.

"He can see your soul if you have one!" isn't just a saying. It's a warning.

## Background

Nobody knows how Grills-bear got his stitches. Some say he was a test subject that escaped. Some say he volunteered to be reconstructed. Some say he's been stitched together so many times that the original bear is long gone.

He doesn't talk about it.

What he DOES talk about is you. How you're doing. What's bothering you. What you're hiding.

He always knows.

---

## Role in the Killaverse

Grills-bear serves as an unexpected morale officer and lie detector. His supportive nature makes him invaluable for keeping spirits high, while his soul-reading ability makes him dangerous to enemies who rely on deception.

**Potential Story Role:**
- The one who sees through Tachyon's manipulation before others
- Emotional support for characters in crisis
- Interrogator who doesn't need to ask questions—just looks
