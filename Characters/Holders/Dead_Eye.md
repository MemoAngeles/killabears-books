## Appearance

Dead Eye got his name the obvious way. One look and you know which eye.

Dark fur over a frame built for punishment — not dodging it, absorbing it. The laser eye is the centerpiece: cybernetic or mutated, nobody's sure, but it glows with targeting precision that makes snipers jealous. The rest of him is equally unsettling — there's an undead quality to his resilience, like damage just... doesn't register the way it should.

He stands like a wall. He fights like a wall that shoots lasers.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Unshakeable — physically and mentally
- Few words, precise actions
- The eye sees everything, judges silently
- Pain is a suggestion he ignores

**The Enforcer:**
Dead Eye doesn't chase. He positions. He locks down a zone and nothing gets through. The laser eye isn't just a weapon — it's a statement. You enter his line of sight, you're already targeted. Whether he fires is up to how smart you are about leaving.

The undead resilience isn't supernatural — it's stubbornness weaponized. He takes hits that would drop other bears and just... keeps standing. It's unnerving.
