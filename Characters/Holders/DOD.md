## Appearance

D.O.D. is what happens when the Ceta Ko curse hits a cub and the cub hits back.

Dark fur stretched over a frame that's more skull than face — massive teeth permanently exposed in a grin that's not friendly. Not even a little. One ear is solid black, the other has a gold earring dangling from it like a trophy from something that used to own it. Bandages wrap his limbs, not for healing — for holding things together.

The bulletproof vest is the only concession to self-preservation. Everything else about D.O.D. says he doesn't care what happens to him as long it happens to you first. Scattered bones around his feet aren't decoration. They're leftovers.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Feral but focused — the curse sharpened him, not scattered him
- Tiny body, enormous violence
- Collects bones. Doesn't explain why.
- The other cubs are scared of him. He doesn't notice.

**The Horror:**
Most cursed bears fight the Ceta Ko. D.O.D. made friends with it. He's a cub-sized nightmare that moves like something twice his size, all exposed teeth and rattling bones. The bandages suggest he's falling apart. He's not. He's just built different.
