## Appearance

Stitchz looks like he was assembled from pieces of other bears — and maybe he was. Patchwork fur in clashing colors, stitched together with visible thread. A purple scarf wraps around his neck, the one consistent piece of his appearance. Every patch tells a story. Not all of them are his.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Resilient — literally held together by stitches
- Dark humor about his own patchwork nature
- Collects pieces of things — memories, objects, allies
- The purple scarf is non-negotiable

**The Patchwork Bear:**
Stitchz doesn't know if he was always like this or if something made him this way. He doesn't care. Every stitch is a lesson, every patch is a survival story. He's been torn apart and put back together so many times that destruction is just... temporary.
