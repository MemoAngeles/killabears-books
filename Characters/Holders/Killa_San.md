## Appearance

Killa-San is what happens when ancient warrior tradition meets Thread-mutated biology. And it's terrifying.

Orange gummy fur — translucent, fire-infused, glowing faintly from the high-density Thread energy that mutated him. You can almost see through him in the right light. The Suzuika-inspired armor is dark navy with orange accents that match his body perfectly, horned helmet with golden crescent crest sitting heavy on a cub-sized frame. The armor shouldn't fit a cub this small. It does. That says something.

The Freddy mask covers his face — white with black X-eyes, a relic from Fomo's era that nobody can explain how he got. Behind it, a triangular orange nose and a gummy body that refuses to stay damaged.

And the sword. Orange gummy crystal, translucent like him, shaped like a blade but alive in the way only gummy material can be. It bends on impact, absorbs force, then snaps back harder.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Silent warrior — lets the sword talk
- Honors the Kenjōkai code despite being a cub
- The mask hides more than a face — it hides an intensity that scares adults
- Disciplined beyond his age, possibly beyond his species

**The Gummy Samurai:**
Killa-San shouldn't exist. Gummy cubs are rare. Gummy cubs who found Fomo-era relics and taught themselves Kenjōkai swordsmanship are impossible. Yet here he sits, tiny and translucent and holding a sword made of his own biology, wearing armor inspired by creatures most bears only read about in scrolls.

The Freddy mask is the mystery. It's from Fomo's era — how did a Primal-born gummy cub end up with a Fomo-era artifact? He doesn't answer. He doesn't talk much at all.

## Lore Notes

- **Orange Gummy** fur is a mutation caused by exposure to high-density Thread energy, specifically fire-infused. These bears have regenerative properties — their bodies slowly reform after damage.
- **Gummy parents** have a 50% chance of producing Gummy offspring. The elemental color is determined by the environment of conception/birth.
- **Suzuika-inspired armor** suggests deep reverence for the ancient creatures. The Kenjōkai tribe studies Suzuikas extensively.
- **The Freddy mask** is a Fomo Era artifact — its presence on a cub raises questions about timeline contamination.
