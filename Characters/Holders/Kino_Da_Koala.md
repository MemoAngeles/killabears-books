## Appearance

Kino is ENERGY personified. Grey-brown koala-style fur, a crisp white bucket hat, and those iconic red/blue 3D glasses that he NEVER takes off—even though literally nothing is in 3D.

His tongue is always out, like he's perpetually tasting the air for vibes. He wears a grey turtleneck sweater that somehow looks both cozy and chaotic.

## The 3D Glasses Mystery

Kino insists the 3D glasses let him see things others can't. "Extra dimensions, bro. You wouldn't understand."

Nobody believes him.

But sometimes he reacts to things that aren't there... and then those things turn out to be real.

Coincidence? Probably. Maybe. Hopefully.
