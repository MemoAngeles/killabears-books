## Appearance

Kaenix is marked. White fur bears the unmistakable X branded across his chest — the mark of the X1 mutation. A red mohawk blazes atop his head like a warning flame. In his paw, a cursed flaming sword burns with fire that never goes out and never stops hurting the one who wields it. He's a cub carrying a weapon that's killing him slowly, and he doesn't care.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- The X mutation made him different. The sword made him dangerous.
- Red mohawk matches his temper
- Carries a curse willingly — the sword chose him
- Young, fierce, burning from the inside

**The Cursed:**
The flaming sword is cursed. It burns the wielder. Kaenix holds it anyway. The X across his chest appeared the day he picked it up, and he hasn't put it down since. Pain is fuel. The mutation is evolution. The mohawk is just style.
