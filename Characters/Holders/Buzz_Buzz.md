## Appearance

Buzz Buzz is the most aggressively cute thing on the MotherBear Ship, and that's saying something for a cub.

Teal fur stuffed into a yellow-and-black bee suit that's slightly too big for him. Antennae bobbing on his head like they've got their own agenda. And then there are the wings — not bee wings, butterfly wings, translucent purple-pink and way too pretty for a combat zone. He's got a pacifier clipped to his suit and a bubblegum bubble that never seems to pop.

He looks like a plush toy someone left in a warzone. That's exactly how he likes it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Deceptively fast — don't let the pacifier fool you
- Curious about everything, afraid of nothing
- Communicates mostly in buzzing sounds and giggles
- Sticky. In every sense of the word.

**The Buzzer:**
Buzz Buzz doesn't understand danger. Not because he's brave — because the concept simply hasn't registered yet. He flies into situations that make veteran bears flinch, blows a bubblegum bubble, and somehow comes out the other side covered in something gross but completely unharmed.

The other cubs follow him because he's fun. The adults watch him because someone has to.
