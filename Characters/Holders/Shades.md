## Appearance

Shades is a fashion disaster weaponized. Pink pixel body, a traffic cone worn as a hat (unironically), rainbow shades that never come off, and a rainbow grill that flashes with every grin. He's absurd. He's obnoxious. He's impossible to ignore, which turns out to be a surprisingly effective tactical advantage.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- The traffic cone is not a joke. Or it is. He won't clarify.
- Rainbow everything — shades, grill, attitude
- Loud, proud, and aggressively visible
- KillaBit who refuses to be background noise

**The Spectacle:**
Shades decided early that if he was going to be a KillaBit, he'd be the most visible KillaBit in existence. Traffic cone. Rainbow accessories. Pink pixels. He's a walking distraction, and in combat, distraction is a weapon.
