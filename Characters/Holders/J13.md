## Appearance

J13 is what goes bump in the night—and knows it.

Deep purple fur contrasts with a bone-white skull mask that covers his entire face. Hollow black eyes stare out from the mask, revealing nothing. He wears a purple KillaBears cap with a bear logo, and his wrists appear bound with white wrappings.

The cheese slice he holds is... unexplained. Nobody asks about the cheese.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**

**The Night Shadow Gang:**

## Background

### "He's What Goes Bump in the Night"

Nobody knows J13's origin. He appeared one night, skull mask already in place, and simply... stayed. The Night Shadow Gang formed around him, drawn to his energy.

Some say he's a ghost. Some say he's a bear who died and came back wrong. Some say he's perfectly normal and just really, REALLY committed to the aesthetic.

J13 doesn't confirm or deny anything.
