## Appearance

Green Goblin is not a bear. Let's get that out of the way first.

Short, wiry, green-skinned, and topped with a flame-red mohawk that adds precious inches to his height. He wears oversized boxing gloves that look comically large on his goblin frame — but there's nothing funny about getting hit by them. His eyes are sharp, calculating, the eyes of a scavenger who's survived the Rift Lands on nothing but cunning and fists.

As an Ursus native, he wasn't summoned here. This is his home. The bears are the visitors.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Scrappy survivor — turns nothing into something
- Street smart, book dumb, fight smart
- Chip on his shoulder the size of a MotherBear
- Loyal once earned, but earning it takes work

**The Scrapper:**
Green Goblin grew up in the margins of Ursus, in the places bears don't bother patrolling. He learned to fight with what he had (fists), eat what he found (everything), and survive what came (also everything). The boxing gloves are his one luxury — and his greatest weapon.
