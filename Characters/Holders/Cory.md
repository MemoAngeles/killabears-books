## Appearance

Cory is bright red fur wrapped in white clothes and quiet sadness. His eyes are large, expressive, and perpetually look like he's just heard disappointing news. He wears a simple white hoodie that makes his red fur pop.

He's objectively good-looking—and he knows it. That's part of the problem.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Romantically hopeful despite constant disappointment
- Self-aware about his situation
- Genuinely kind
- Looks for connection in a disconnected world

**The Paradox:**
Cory looks great. He knows he looks great. Everyone tells him he looks great. But looking great hasn't helped him find what he's actually looking for—real connection. A perfect bear mate.

"He's bummed because he looks so dope and hasn't found his perfect bear mate."

---

## Background

### The Search

Cory has tried everything. Dating apps. Chance encounters. Being set up by friends. Joining clubs. Attending events.

Nothing works.

It's not that bears aren't interested—they are. It's that none of them are RIGHT. Cory is looking for something specific, something real, and he refuses to settle for less.

Some call it picky. Cory calls it knowing his worth.

---

## Philosophy

- Better to be alone than with the wrong one
- Love should feel like coming home
- Looking good is easy; being good is hard
- Hope is a choice you make every day

## Role in the Killaverse

Cory represents the romantic heart—the belief that connection matters even in dark times. Perfect for:
- The emotional center of a group
- Subplot about finding love in unexpected places
- Character who sees the best in others
- Reminder that some fights are personal, not political

**Potential Story Arc:**
Cory joins the resistance not for political reasons, but because he believes in the PEOPLE fighting. Maybe he finds his perfect match among the rebels. Maybe he learns that the search itself has value.
