## Appearance

Candy Crush is chaos wrapped in candy. Purple gummy fur glistens like sugar coating. A candyskull mask covers his face — Calaverna tradition meets sweet nightmare. A rocket backpack strapped to his tiny frame provides terrifying mobility. And in his paw? A clown's mace. Because of course.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Hyperactive, sugar-fueled destruction
- Calaverna traditions twisted through a candy lens
- The rocket backpack isn't safe. He doesn't care.
- Regenerative — you can't keep a good gummy down

**The Sweet Nightmare:**
Candy Crush is what happens when Calaverna's death traditions meet a cub with unlimited energy and a rocket strapped to his back. He's sweet, sticky, explosive, and he regenerates. Every fight with Candy Crush is a sugar rush of violence.
