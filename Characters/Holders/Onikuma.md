## Appearance

Onikuma is small, green, blindfolded, and radiating danger. Green gummy fur glows faintly beneath plasma armor that shifts and crackles with energy. A blindfold covers eyes that don't need light. Beside him floats a plasma skull companion — a ghostly construct that moves with its own terrible intelligence.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Silent and focused despite being a cub
- The plasma skull is his voice, his weapon, his friend
- Blindfolded — sees through the plasma skull's eyes
- Ancient warrior trapped in a cub's body

**The Demon Cub:**
Onikuma means "demon bear" and he earns the name. The blindfold isn't weakness — it's control. Without visual input, all his energy channels through the plasma armor and the skull companion. He's a battery of destruction wrapped in gummy cub fur.
