## Appearance

Lord Beardinand 3Deadly carries himself like old money that learned to kill. Dark brown fur, immaculately groomed despite the wilderness he stalks through, frames a face that's equal parts nobleman and apex predator. The 3D glasses aren't a fashion choice — they're proprietary tech, scanning dimensional frequencies most bears can't perceive. His hunter's kit hangs from a weathered bandolier, each tool positioned for instant access.

There's something unsettling about how still he can stand. Like a statue with teeth.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Methodical and patient — the hunt is a ritual, not a scramble
- Aristocratic manners masking feral instincts
- Catalogues every species he encounters with obsessive detail
- Polite right up until the moment he isn't

**The Hunter:**
Beardinand doesn't chase prey. He studies it, learns its patterns, understands its nature — then closes the distance before it knows he's there. The "3Deadly" isn't a boast. It's a body count methodology.
