## Appearance

ScooterBear is a Day of the Dead party on legs. DOD-patterned fur in vivid colors, a yellow beanie pulled down tight, and iconic red-and-blue 3D glasses that he swears let him "see the real world." He's bright, loud, and impossible to miss — which is either his greatest asset or biggest liability depending on the mission.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Upbeat, energetic, always moving
- The 3D glasses are his whole identity
- Sees the world differently — literally and figuratively
- The holder behind Knox, Terry, Frutti Tutti Freddie, and El Duque

**The Scooter:**
ScooterBear doesn't walk anywhere. He bounces, slides, and zooms. Named for his speed and agility, he's the bear you send when you need something done fast and don't mind if it's done loud.
