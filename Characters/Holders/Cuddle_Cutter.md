## Appearance

Cuddle Cutter is digital violence made adorable. Pink pixel body glitches and shifts at the edges. Rainbow grills flash when she smiles — which is always, especially during combat. The ice sword in her hand shouldn't exist in a digital being, but there it is: cold, crystalline, and very real.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Cute name, deadly execution
- The cuddle is a lie. The cutter is the truth.
- Pixelated existence gives her a glitchy, unpredictable nature
- Rainbow grills are her signature — flash before the cut

**The Cutter:**
Don't let the name fool you. "Cuddle" is ironic. The sword is not. Cuddle Cutter exists in the space between digital and physical, wielding an ice blade that freezes what it touches. She's adorable until she isn't.
