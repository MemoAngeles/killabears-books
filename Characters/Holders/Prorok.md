## Appearance

Prorok looks like he stepped out of the Primal Era itself. Brown fur weathered by survival. A red mask/goggles covering his upper face—protection from cave hazards and whatever else lurks in Killa Caves.

He wears a leopard print loincloth and not much else. No fancy equipment. No technology. Just the essentials.

And beside him, always, a young DinoCub—learning by walking, not by instruction.

## Background

### Killa Caves

Deep in the Killaverse, there's a place few Bears stay long enough to understand.

**Killa Caves.**

Most who enter, leave quickly. Or don't leave at all.

Prorok stayed. He endured. He learned.

### The Red Mask

"The red mask he wears doesn't grant crazy power... it carries wisdom left behind by those who didn't make it through."

Every mark on that mask represents a lesson. Every scratch is someone's last teaching. Prorok wears the accumulated wisdom of the fallen.

## The DinoCub

Along his journeys, Prorok found a lost DinoCub—confused, curious, chasing everything. Instead of instructing, he simply walks beside it.

"Learning not through instruction, but by walking beside him."

The DinoCub represents Prorok's teaching method: presence over lectures.
