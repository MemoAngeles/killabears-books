## Appearance

Neon Reaper wears death as art. His fur blazes with Day of the Dead patterns — flowers, skulls, and bright neon colors painted across dark fur in the tradition of Calaverna. He is beautiful and terrifying, a walking altar to the departed. Every color means something. Every pattern tells a story of someone who didn't make it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Spiritual connection to death and the departed
- From Miktah/Calaverna — carries that region's traditions
- Quiet reverence mixed with absolute lethality
- Honors the dead by becoming death itself

**The Reaper:**
In Calaverna, death isn't feared — it's celebrated. Neon Reaper embodies this philosophy. He doesn't kill with anger. He kills with ceremony. Every fallen enemy gets a moment of respect. Then he moves on.
