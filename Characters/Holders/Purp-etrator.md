## Appearance

Beige fur, purple ski mask. That's all you get. Purp-etrator doesn't do introductions — the mask says everything. A member of the Ski-Mafia (also known as the Ski Mask Gang), he moves in shadows and speaks in actions. The purple mask is his identity. Without it, he's nobody. With it, he's a problem.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Mysterious, identity hidden behind the mask
- Ski Mask Gang loyalty runs deep
- Calculated criminal turned resistance fighter
- The perpetrator of things best left unspoken

**The Mask:**
Purp-etrator joined the Ski Mask Gang for the same reason anyone does — the world gave him nothing, so he took. But Bearington's regime is worse than any street hustle. Now the gang's skills serve a higher purpose. Same masks. Different targets.
