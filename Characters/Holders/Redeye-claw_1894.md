## Appearance

Redeye-claw 1894 is a KillaBit who's been through war and came back part machine. Black fur is scarred and battle-worn. Red eyes glow with digital intensity. One arm is cybernetic — a robot replacement that's stronger than what it replaced. The "1894" designation suggests he's been catalogued, numbered, filed. He doesn't care about the file. He cares about the claw.
