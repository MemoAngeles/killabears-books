## Appearance

Agent 1968 looks like a bear that's seen something that can't be unseen. The hollow gaze — not blank, not empty, but *hollow*, like looking into rooms with no floors — sits in a face otherwise built for violence. Dark fur, lean frame, every muscle tuned for speed and lethality.

The razor fangs are always slightly visible, not from aggression but from a mouth that never fully closes. Something's not right behind those eyes. Everyone can feel it. Nobody asks about it.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Silent and efficient — speaks only when tactically necessary
- The hollow gaze unnerves allies and enemies equally
- Operates on instinct more than strategy — feral, not feral-stupid
- Something happened to Agent 1968. He doesn't talk about it.

**The Operative:**
Feral Operative isn't a rank — it's a warning label. 1968 operates in the space between controlled and unhinged, a razor edge where his feral instincts serve the mission. When he goes quiet, that's when you worry.
