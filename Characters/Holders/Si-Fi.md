## Appearance

Si-Fi gleams like a relic from Ursus's golden age.

Young but armored like a war veteran, this KillaCub wears golden bear armor forged in the Primal traditions — a helmet that frames fierce eyes, gauntlets that catch the light like small suns, and a war medallion that marks him as blessed by Ursoth himself. His yellow-golden fur seems to glow beneath the armor, as if the Primal energy runs through his very being.

As a Primal Native, Si-Fi was never summoned through the MotherBear — he was born here, in Ursus, and carries the old magic in his blood.

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Steadfast and protective
- Deeply spiritual — connected to Ursoth's legacy
- Young but carries ancient responsibility
- Quiet strength over loud bravado

**The Guardian:**
Si-Fi doesn't fight for glory. He fights because the Primal traditions demand that those who can protect, must protect. Despite being a cub, he carries himself with the gravity of someone who understands what's at stake. The golden armor isn't decoration — it's a covenant.
