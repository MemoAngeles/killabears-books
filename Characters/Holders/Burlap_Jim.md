## Appearance

Burlap Jim is a walking horror story. Dark brown fur disappears beneath a burlap sack mask — rough, stitched, with crude eye holes that glow faintly. He carries a scythe too big for a cub, dragging it with grim determination. And perched on his shoulder? A baby Fire Suzuika — a tiny, flame-wreathed alien creature that provides the only warmth in his otherwise cold presence.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Silent and eerie — the burlap mask never comes off
- The Fire Suzuika is his only companion, his only warmth
- Carries the scythe like it was born in his paw
- A nightmare who just wants to be left alone

**The Scarecrow:**
Burlap Jim didn't choose the horror aesthetic. Or maybe he did. The burlap mask appeared and never left. The scythe felt right. The Fire Suzuika chose him, sensing something worth warming beneath all that darkness. He's terrifying, but the baby alien on his shoulder suggests there's more to the story.
