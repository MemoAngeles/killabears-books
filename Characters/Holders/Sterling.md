## Appearance

$terlin looks like money made angry.

Olive-brown fur, immaculately groomed. Pink-red eyes that have never once looked friendly. Always in a perfectly tailored gray suit. And around his neck: a gold chain so thick it could anchor a ship, with a dollar sign pendant that catches the light every time he moves.

He conducts business from a high-rise office overlooking the city—not Bearington Tower high, but high enough to see everything that matters.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- All business, all the time
- Believes everything has a price
- Patient until he isn't
- Never raises his voice (doesn't need to)

**The $terlin Way:**
$terlin doesn't get angry. He gets even. He doesn't threaten. He calculates. Every interaction is a transaction, and he always comes out ahead.

---

## Background

### Before Bearington

$terlin built The Syndicate from nothing—or so the story goes. Some say he was Lowtown born, clawed his way up through every dirty job until he owned the bears giving the orders. Others say he was always wealthy, just smart enough to invest in the shadows instead of the light.

What everyone agrees on: when Bearington took over, $terlin was already too embedded to remove. The new regime needed his networks. He needed their blind eye. An arrangement was made.

### The Arrangement

$terlin doesn't challenge Bearington's authority. Bearington doesn't look too closely at Lowtown commerce. Both profit. Neither trusts the other.

$terlin is the undisputed boss. He:
