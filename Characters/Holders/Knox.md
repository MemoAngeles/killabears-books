## Appearance

Knox is chaos wearing heart-shaped sunglasses.

Bright red fur, a grin that's way too wide, and those iconic pink heart-shaped sunglasses that somehow make him look MORE unhinged. His tongue often lolls out with excitement, and there's usually drool involved.

But the most distinctive feature? The blue Suzuika perched on his head—a one-eyed octopus-like creature that seems to share his enthusiasm for mayhem.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Loud and reckless
- Lives for chaos
- Doesn't know when to back down
- Fiercely loyal once he decides you're worth it

**The Enforcer:**
"Knox is a loud, reckless enforcer who lives for chaos and doesn't know when to back down. In the Killaverse, he's the first one through the door heading to the action."

He doesn't wait. He doesn't plan. He MOVES.

---

## The Suzuika Sidekick

Knox's blue Suzuika is a permanent companion—a blessing from the cyclopean aliens that somehow found the perfect host. The Suzuika:
- Spooks enemies with its alien appearance
- Confuses targets (is that an OCTOPUS on his HEAD?)
- Provides strange alien perception
- Seems just as chaos-hungry as Knox

Together, they're a two-entity wrecking ball.

## Role in the Killaverse

Knox is the berserker—the one you unleash when subtlety has failed. Perfect for:
- Shock troops and door-kicking
- Distraction while others execute plans
- Comic relief through sheer chaos
- The friend who's exhausting but irreplaceable

**Potential Story Role:**
Every team needs a Knox. Someone who'll charge in without hesitation. Someone who makes the enemy focus on HIM while the smart bears do the real work.

He knows his role. He loves his role.
