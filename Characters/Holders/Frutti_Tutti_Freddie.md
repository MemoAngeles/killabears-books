## Appearance

Frutti Tutti Freddie is a contradiction — rainbow colors and a horror-movie hockey mask. Colorful fur bursts in every direction, a purple scarf adds flair, and a yellow beanie sits on top. But the FreddyBear hockey mask turns all that color into something menacing. He's a carnival and a nightmare having a playdate.

---

## Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Playful exterior, dangerous interior
- The mask comes ON when things get serious
- Loves color, hates boredom
- Part entertainer, part enforcer

**The Freddie:**
When the mask is off, Freddie is the life of the party — fruit-colored, loud, laughing. When the mask goes on, the laughing doesn't stop. That's the scary part.
