## Appearance

Dark Star is a punk-rock bear with peachy-pink skin, long teal/cyan hair, and sharp fangs with an underbite snarl. She has lime-green pointed ears (or horn-like protrusions), an X-shaped scar on her forehead, and a permanently defiant grin.

**Gear:**
- **Demonic rock guitar** — pink electric bass with an angular, aggressive body and a green skull-shaped headstock
- **Black punk jacket/vest** with studded details
- **Headphones** — black with glowing green ear cups, worn over one ear
- **Green skull necklace/choker**
- **Studded bracelets/cuffs** with pink rivets
- Sharp white claws
