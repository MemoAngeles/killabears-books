## Appearance

Shinobi is a bear fully encased in dark gunmetal-gray and black mechanized ninja armor. Only the white dome of his head is visible through the helmet. His narrow angular red visor gives him an aggressive, unreadable expression.

**Armor:**
- **Full-body dark gray/black mech suit** — segmented plating covering everything
- **Red circular nodes/lights** on chest, wrists, hands, and feet
- **Blade-like ear/wing fins** protruding from helmet sides — bat or insect-like silhouette
- **Circular red ear-pieces/sensors** on sides of head
- **Smooth white dome** helmet top with mechanical seam lines

**Weapon:**
- **Large silver shuriken** (throwing star) — metallic with circular center hole
- Likely has more hidden weapons within the armor

## Powers

- **Stealth/invisibility** — ninja training enhanced by tech armor
- **Shuriken mastery** — precision ranged attacks
- **Mech-enhanced agility** — the armor boosts already exceptional ninja speed
- **Sensor array** — the red nodes likely provide enhanced awareness, targeting, heat vision

## Lore Connection

- **Fomo Era (Modern)** — active ninja superhero in the current timeline

- The modern-era counterpart to the ancient warriors — where Goat/Kaeru use traditional weapons, Shinobi uses tech-enhanced ninja gear
- Mecha-ninja aesthetic — blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology
