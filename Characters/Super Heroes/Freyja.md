## Appearance

Freyja is a fierce feline warrior with white/cream fur and golden-orange tabby markings. She has a wide fanged grin, sharp teeth, and one visible purple eye with a rainbow shimmer.

**Gear:**
- **Viking-style horned helmet** with two large curved brown horns
- Helmet features a **glowing blue skull emblem** with a heart on its forehead
- **Stone/metal face guard** partially covering one eye
- **Crescent battle axe** with a rainbow/iridescent sheen blade
- Axe has a gold crossguard, red gems, pink and teal wrappings, heart motif, and rainbow-striped grip
- **Long, flowing golden-blonde wavy hair** cascading down, braided near the bottom
- **Dark plaid clothing** (tunic/skirt)
- **Teal gem necklace**
