## Appearance

Jag is a spotted jaguar wearing sleek, futuristic high-tech armor. Tan/sandy base fur with dark brown spots across body and tail. Lighter cream-colored muzzle and underbelly. Wide fanged grin — fierce but adorable.

**Armor:**
- **Golden leopard-print helmet** with glowing cyan/turquoise visor
- Diamond-shaped **cyan gem** on forehead
- **Dark gray/gunmetal body armor** with cyan gem accents on chest and limbs
- **White blade-like shoulder pauldrons** — weaponized protrusions
- Bladed tail attachment with cyan gem
- Sharp white claws on feet
- Sleek, futuristic sci-fi design with glowing teal energy throughout
