## Appearance

Mariachi is a skeleton bear — literally just bones, no flesh. His oversized skull has a heart-shaped nasal cavity, flaming red-orange eyes with yellow fire wisps, and a permanent menacing teeth-baring grin.

**Outfit:**
- **Dark navy/charcoal mariachi suit** with gold embroidered trim
- **White dress shirt** underneath
- **Red bow tie** at the collar
- **Wide-brimmed sombrero** in dark gray/charcoal with lighter gray top and zigzag stitching

**Weapon/Instrument:**
- **Guitar made of bone** — skull-shaped body with orange marigold pom-pom decorations
- Standard strings — it's a real, playable instrument
- The music has POWER

---

## Powers

- **Death magic** — supernatural control over death-related forces
- **Sonic/musical attacks** — the bone guitar channels death energy through music
- **Undead resilience** — already dead, hard to kill (again)
- **Día de los Muertos connection** — bridges the world of the living and dead

---

## Lore Connection

- **Linked to Arcturus** — the death/spirit realm in KillaBears lore
- From a **Primal Era land mirroring Mexico**
- Supernatural origin — not tech-based, not mutation-based; truly undead/spiritual
- The marigold decorations on his guitar are traditional offerings to the dead
- Could serve as a guide between worlds, or a terrifying combatant, or both

## Personality

- Menacing, confident, theatrical
- Death doesn't make him grim — he's a PERFORMER
- Punk/rock attitude blended with traditional culture
- Aggressive but with style — everything he does has flair
