## Appearance

Triton is an ancient SeaBear — dark teal/deep blue-green skin with a smooth, rounded form. His most unsettling feature: **he has no visible mouth or nose**. Only two massive, luminous **glowing turquoise eyes** dominate his face, giving him an alien, unknowable presence.

**Armor:**
- **Ornate golden armor** with intricate scrollwork patterns — similar origin/style to what **Ursoth** wore
- **Crowned helmet/mask** framing the face with a cyan fleur-de-lis emblem on the forehead
- **Chest plate** with a central glowing cyan gem
- **Golden scale-mail** details on the lower body
- The armor's golden design mirrors the ancient divine armor seen on Ursoth — suggesting shared origin or era

**Gear:**
- **Golden staff/scepter** topped with a decorative pointed head featuring a glowing cyan/turquoise orb
- **Small bat-like or fin-like teal wings** on the back
- **Teal-green boots**
- **Glowing cyan mouth/grin** — the glow suggests communication through energy, not speech

---

## Powers

- **Telekinesis** — moves objects with his mind, channeling **Suzuika psychic power**
- **Suzuika Blessing** — the glowing turquoise eyes are the mark of a bear who has bonded with a Suzuika
  - Per lore: "Bears who form deep bonds with the Suzuika may receive their blessing—marked by glowing turquoise eyes. This represents a partial merging of consciousness with the Suzuika."
- **Aquatic mastery** — as a SeaBear, commands the deep waters
- **Psychic communication** — no mouth, communicates through Suzuika-granted telepathy
- **Ancient knowledge** — has existed since before modern civilization

---

## Lore Connection

- **SeaBear** — an ancient aquatic sub-species of bear, possibly pre-dating surface KillaBears
- **Golden armor matches Ursoth's** — suggests Triton is from the same ancient era, possibly a contemporary or servant of the ancient spirits
- **Suzuika-blessed** — his turquoise eyes confirm a deep bond with the alien Suzuika race
  - Suzuika are "ancient alien race of cyclopean octopi who have existed since before bears walked Ursus"
  - The blessing grants telekinesis and partial merging of consciousness
- His lack of mouth/nose may be a result of the Suzuika transformation — more alien than bear now
- Could be one of the most ancient and powerful characters in the entire lore

---

## Personality

- Unknowable, ancient, alien
- Communicates without speaking — telepathy or psychic projection
- Regal bearing — the golden armor and scepter suggest authority
- More Suzuika than bear at this point — operates on a different level than mortal characters
