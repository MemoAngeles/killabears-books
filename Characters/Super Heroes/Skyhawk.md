## Appearance

Skyhawk is a brown-furred female bear with a cream/beige face and a cheeky fanged grin. She wears a bright orange aviator jacket with brown belt straps and an "01" marking.

**Gear:**
- **Mechanical wings** — dark metallic turbine/jet-engine blade wings in gunmetal gray, mounted on an orange mechanical base on her back
- **Aviator goggles** — large, silver/gray frames with dark tinted lenses and an orange strap with X-stitch detail, pushed up on forehead
- **Over-ear headphones** — silver/gray, for comms
- **Orange flight jacket** with brown belt buckles
- **Glowing yellow-green circular paw pads** — tech-enhanced, possibly for energy projection or grip

---

## Powers

- **Mechanical flight** — jet-turbine wings provide high-speed aerial combat capability
- **Aerial superiority** — built for speed, maneuverability, and dogfighting
- **Tech-enhanced paws** — glowing pads suggest energy projection or enhanced grip for mid-air combat
- **Comms/coordination** — headphones suggest she's a team coordinator in the air
