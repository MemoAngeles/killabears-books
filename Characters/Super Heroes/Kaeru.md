## Appearance

Kaeru is a bright green, goblin-like creature with sharp teeth, a wide toothy grin, and a cocky swagger. He wears a leather eye patch with a white X-stitch over one eye and has a skull-shaped belt buckle.

**Gear:**
- **Oversized red hooded cloak/cape** — dramatic, billowing
- **Dark navy/black outfit** with light blue accents underneath
- **Wrapped club/mallet** — dark wrapped grip with red rope/cord and golden pommel cap
- **Mismatched sneakers** — one green/yellow, one red
- **Eye patch** with X-stitch (covering one eye)
