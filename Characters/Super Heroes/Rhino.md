## Appearance

Rhino is a massive rhinoceros in heavy rhino-themed mech armor. Dark gray/charcoal skin with a lighter gray underbelly. Sharp fangs, menacing grin, and glowing yellow eyes.

**Armor (Rhino Mech Suit):**
- **Red and gunmetal plate armor** — heavy, industrial, built for impact
- **Viking-style horned helmet** with a large white central horn (his natural horn reinforced with armor) and two curved side horns
- **Red armored shoulder pauldrons** and chest plate with a red gem/crystal
- **Red leg armor/greaves** with visible clawed toes
- The suit amplifies his already considerable natural strength

**Weapon:**
- **Double-headed battle axe** — silver blades with a red spiked morningstar hub at center, yellow/orange gem, gray handle with yellow rivets
- Massive, devastating, swung with rhinoceros strength

---

## Powers

- **Superhuman strength** — rhinoceros base + mech suit amplification
- **Armored durability** — the mech suit makes him nearly indestructible
- **Devastating melee combat** — the battle axe combined with a charging rhino is terrifying
- **Charge attack** — rhinoceros instincts, horn-first
