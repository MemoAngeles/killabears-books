## Appearance

Kahuna looks like a living, oversized tiki totem — brown wood-grain body with visible knots and spiral carvings. He has glowing yellow eyes, a red bulbous nose, and a wide toothy grin. Coconut-like round ears frame his massive head.

**Adornments:**
- **Tropical flowers** covering his head — pink hibiscus, orange/red lilies, purple/magenta blossoms, yellow feather-like plumes
- **Green tropical leaves** surrounding his body
- **Painted facial markings** — yellow zigzag patterns on forehead and chin, red stripes, blue outlines around mouth/jaw, spiral carvings near eyes
- **Green grass/leaf skirt** (hula skirt) tied with braided rope belt
- **Gem decorations** near feet — red/green jewels, white feather puffs

## Powers

- **Divine/godlike abilities** — Kahuna is a GOD, not just a warrior
- **Nature manipulation** — connected to tropical plant life, ocean, volcanic forces
- **Spiritual authority** — commands respect from all living things in his domain
- **Ancient beyond measure** — existed since before most civilizations

- **Primal Era native** — from a land mirroring **Polynesia**
