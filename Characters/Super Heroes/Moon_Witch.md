## Appearance

Moon Witch is a white-furred female bear with pale lavender-pink hair and glowing purple eyes. She wraps her lower face in dark ninja-style bandages/scarf, giving her a mysterious, veiled appearance. She wears a white hooded cloak with bear ears and a purple zigzag crown/tiara.

**Gear:**
- **Ornate magical staff/key** — crescent moon shape with red gems and pearl-like orbs (carried by one of her ghost companions)
- **Dark body wrappings/bandages** underneath the cloak
- **Belt/chain** with circular ornaments
- **Mauve/dusty pink boots**
- **Purple lightning bolt motifs** emanating from her crown

**Ghost Companions:**
- **Three ghost bear spirits** — white, blob-like entities with big dark eyes and wide toothy grins
- They surround her playfully, acting as both companions and weapons
- She appears brooding/grumpy despite their cheerful energy

---

## Powers

- **Spirit Awakening** — can wake and call forth spirits of the dead
- **Spirit Control** — commands ghost bears and other spirits
- **Necromantic magic** — bridges the living and dead worlds
- **Crescent moon connection** — her power is tied to lunar cycles
- **Purple lightning** — magical energy discharge

---

## Lore Connection

- **Primal Era native** — an ancient spirit-magic user
- Her power to wake spirits could connect to Arcturus (the death/spirit realm)
- The ghost bears she commands may be spirits of fallen warriors
- Moon connection is interesting given Ursus IS a moon — her power may be tied to the land itself
- Could be an ally or neutral mystic encountered in Primal Era stories

---

## Personality

- Brooding, mysterious, veiled
- Grumpy despite the cheerful ghosts around her — they're her companions, not her mood
- Powerful and knows it
- Keeps to herself — the bandages/mask suggest she hides more than her face
