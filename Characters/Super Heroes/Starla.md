## Appearance

Starla is a brown-furred female fox with cream/white muzzle markings, large amber eyes, and a fluffy cream-and-brown tail. Her most striking feature is her **cosmic hair** — voluminous dark teal/navy blue waves filled with scattered golden stars, like she's carrying a piece of the night sky.

**Gear:**
- **Golden ornamental tiara/headpiece** with swirl designs and a red gem at the forehead
- **White feathered wings** — small pairs near the ears and on the back, angelic in appearance
- **Pink magic wand** with a golden 3D star topper and red gem near the handle
- **White and teal outfit** with star-patterned details and a teal bow at the collar
- **Golden belt/buckle**
- **Cross-shaped bandage** on the forehead (under tiara)
