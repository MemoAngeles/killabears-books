## Appearance

DeltaBear is a blue-furred bear encased in a massive orange-and-silver mech suit. The suit has a rounded bear-head helmet with a wide transparent visor, mechanical goggles/sensor lenses mounted on each ear (glowing yellow-green), and heavy armored boots with glowing circular lights.

His most striking feature: **arcs of cyan-blue and yellow electricity** constantly crackling around his body — from the ears, sides, arms, and feet. A blue jet thruster fires from one arm.

**Key detail**: The suit doesn't just protect him — it **constrains and channels his powers**. Without it, his energy would be uncontrollable. The suit IS the control mechanism.

---

## Powers

- **Electrokinesis** — electric/plasma attacks, EMP bursts
- **Flight/Jet propulsion** — arm-mounted thrusters
- **Heavy mech defense** — armored suit provides extreme durability
- **Enhanced sensors** — goggle devices on ears provide scanning/targeting
- **Raw power** — the suit constrains what would otherwise be devastating, uncontrolled energy

---

## Personality

- Powerful but restrained — knows what happens if the suit comes off
- Protective instincts
- The suit gives him a lumbering, heavy presence despite the bear underneath being small and cute
