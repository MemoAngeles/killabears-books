# The World of Ursus

## Creation

Ursus was born from chaos, shaped by the will of Vethara, the spirit weaver. She stood at the edge of the void and wove the threads of existence into form. From her first breath came the sky; from her tears, the oceans; from her heartbeat, the pulse of life itself.

## The Five Great Spirits

### Ursoth Shadowmaw (Fire)
The firstborn, flame given form. His roar shattered mountains and birthed volcanoes. Wild and untamable, Ursoth represents raw power and transformation.

### Arcturus Bone-Walker (Ice)
Born of the frozen silence between stars. Arcturus walks the line between life and death, keeper of ancient memories preserved in ice.

### Grimheart the Eternal (Lightning)
Storm incarnate, Grimheart's fury lights the sky and shakes the earth. They embody raw energy and the spark of innovation.

### Voidclaw Thornback (Nature)
The forest's guardian, Voidclaw nurtures and destroys in equal measure. Where they walk, life flourishes—and reclaims what was taken.

### Elderfrost the Forgotten (Void)
The youngest and most mysterious. Elderfrost guards the spaces between, the quiet darkness that holds all possibilities.

## The Primal Elements

| Element | Spirit | Characteristics |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| Fire | Ursoth | Passion, destruction, transformation |
| Ice | Arcturus | Preservation, memory, stillness |
| Lightning | Grimheart | Energy, change, innovation |
| Nature | Voidclaw | Growth, decay, renewal |
| Void | Elderfrost | Mystery, potential, the unknown |

## Peoples of Ursus

### KillaBears
The dominant species, blessed (or cursed) by the spirits with elemental affinities.

### Thunderbirds
Servants of Grimheart, masters of the storm.

### Goblins
Small, cunning creatures born from the dust and shadows. Incredibly adaptable.

### Seabears
Ocean dwellers with ties to ancient, forgotten powers.

### The Bone Pickers
Followers of Arcturus, collectors of the dead's wisdom.

### The Nemili
Mysterious forest dwellers aligned with Voidclaw.

### The Ossuary
Skeletal beings that serve the cycle of death and rebirth.

### The Myst Folk
Ethereal beings who exist between realms.

### The Suzuika
An ancient, proud people with deep spiritual traditions.
